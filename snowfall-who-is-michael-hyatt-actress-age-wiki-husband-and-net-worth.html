Snowfall: Who Is Michael Hyatt Actress? Age Revealed

Snowfall actress, Michael Hyatt, is 52 years of age as of 2022. She was born on 17th February 2022 in Handsworth, Birmingham, England, the United Kingdom.

The actress’ real name is Charlene Hyatt. She used the name “Michael” since high school when she was nicknamed for being a fan of American entertainer Michael Jackson.

Since childhood, Hyatt was exposed to art and theatre firm the work of both her parents during the 1970s in Jamaica. When she was 10 years old, she moved to the United States with her mother and two siblings.

The family lived in Maryland and then Washington D.C. Michael received a degree in Bachelor of Fine Arts from Howard University and her Master of Fine Arts from New York University’s Tisch School of the Arts.

During her college days, Hyatt did a theatre practicum and worked at Arena Stage in Washington D.C. Likewise, she worked for director Spike Lee as an assistant during her grad school.
