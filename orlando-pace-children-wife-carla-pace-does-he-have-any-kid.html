Orlando Pace Children &amp; Wife Carla Pace: Does He Have Any Kid?

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Orlando Pace, a former NFL lineman, is living a blissful married life with his wife, Carla Pace. The couple has four children, three sons and one daughter. The seven-time Pro Bowler is never bashful in expressing his love for his family. During his 2016 Pro Football Hall of Fame speech, the inductee expressed his love for his mother and wife. His children all attended Ohio State University, his alma mater. His three boys all played football, albeit his oldest son, Justin, now aspires to be a business executive. Orlando Lamar Pace (born November 4, 1975) is a Sandusky, Ohio native who spent 13 seasons in the NFL, mostly with the St. Louis Rams (1997-2008). In the 1997 NFL draft, he was selected first overall by the franchise.

He won Super Bowl XXXIV with the Rams, joining a group that included Kurt Warner, Fred Miller, Justin Watson, and Isaac Bruce, to mention a few. He played for the Chicago Bears in the 2009 NFL season before retiring. Even though he was terrified of football as a boy, it would later inspire him. As a consequence, he excelled in football at Sandusky High School and then at Ohio State. He is managing his work and family life after retirement. He has a stake in St. Louis radio station KFNS-AM and the TITLE Boxing Club.

Carla Pace, Orlando Pace’s Wife: Relationship Details

Carla Pace, the former Rams player’s wife of almost two decades, resides in St. Louis, Missouri. They married in 2004 and will have been married for 19 years as of 2023. On June 13, they will celebrate their wedding anniversary. Pace’s wife, Carla, uploaded a photo of the couple on their anniversary in June 2019 and commented, “‘Another year down & forever to go.” “Congratulations on your anniversary.” According to reports, the pair met in St. Louis in 1997.

Orlando was a rookie with the NFL franchise Rams at the time, while Carla was a corporate employee who commuted between Cleveland and St. Louis. After two years of dating, his wife became pregnant with their first kid in 1999. As a result, she quit her corporate career and became a full-time stay-at-home mom. They eventually had four children.

Orlando’s Wife’s Job: What Does She Do?

Carla Pace (born December 7, 1974) is the co-founder of the health and fitness business Noor Fit Style. She has an AAFA certification as a group fitness instructor. She is a native of Washington, DC, according to an October 2022 Instagram post by Women Of The NFL. She graduated from Woodrow Wilson Senior High School in 1992. Pace received his Industrial Engineering degree from North Carolina A&T State University in 1996.

She then finished Weatherhead’s Executive MBA program at Case Western Reserve University in 1999. Carla worked with General Electric from 1996 until 2000. She then worked as a project manager at Sourcing.

Carla’s Father of Four Children

The former NFL athlete has four children: three boys (Justin, Jalen, and Landon) and a girl, Kendall. Justin, the oldest, was born on July 19, 2000, and is currently 23 years old. He played football at Ohio State, following in his father’s footsteps, but eventually chose managerial positions. According to his LinkedIn page, he is a 2022 Sports Industry graduate from Ohio. He works as a marketing and fan experience intern in the Department of Athletics at The Ohio State University.

Jalen Orland (born November 6, 2002) is a linebacker for his father’s alma school, Ohio State University. After attending Mary Institute and Saint Louis Country Day School, he joined there in 2021. Kendall, the former football player’s third kid, is also an Ohio State student. Landon Pace was born on July 31, 2006, as the youngest of four children. He is a St. Louis University High School student-athlete and member of the 2025 recruiting class. He visited the Buckeyes in early June 2022 and mentioned that playing for his father’s alma school was a goal of his. He was admitted into the University’s football program by late June of that year.
