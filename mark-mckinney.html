Mark McKinney Bio | Wiki

Mark McKinney (full name is Mark Douglas Brown McKinney) is a Canadian comedian and actor best recognized as a member of the sketch comedy troupe The Kids in the Hall.

He served as a cast member on Saturday Night Live from 1995 to 1997, and from 2003 to 2006. Mark has made appearances in films such as Room for Rent as Warren Baldwin (2017), Snow Cake as the Neighbour (2006), and The Ladies Man as Mr. White (2000).

Additionally, he has voice acted on several sitcoms including Dynaman (1988), Clerks (2001), Rocket Monkeys (2013), and many others. In recent years, Mark has appeared on NBC’s Superstore as the store manager Glenn Sturgis. During his free time, Mark loves reading, writing, and traveling.

Mark McKinney The Kids In The Hall

The Kids in the Hall is a Canadian sketch comedy troupe made up of comedians Kevin McDonald, Dave Foley, Bruce McCulloch, Mark McKinney, and Scott Thompson. The troupe has been active since it was formed in 1984. From 1989 to 1995, they ran a comedy show with the same name on CBC. Additionally, the show aired also on CBS, HBO, and Comedy Central, in the United States of America. In 1996, they released a comedy film titled Brian Candy.
