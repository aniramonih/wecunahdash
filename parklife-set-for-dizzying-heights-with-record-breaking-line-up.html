Parklife Set For Dizzying Heights with Record Breaking Line-Up

Publish date: 2024-08-09

The tenth edition of Parklife Festival is set to take place at Heaton Park, Manchester on Saturday 8th and Sunday 9th June with a hard hitting roster of world class artists confirmed.

Superstars like Cardi B, Christine & The Queens, Solange and Khalid will take to one of Parklife Festival’s 16 stages. Crowd favourites Major Lazer Soundsystem, Stefflon Don, Disclosure and Mark Ronson are also set to make this year’s event the biggest to date.

Parklife unites audiences with eclectic tastes in music, catering for fans of hip-hop, rap, pop, indie and electronic music. Whilst the weather at this Manchester festival is always unpredictable, we at CelebMix love that this event attracts a whole roster of festival fashion style from the boho-chic to the city-slicker-hippie.

DJs Annie Mac and Eric Prydz are among the many tipped to keep the party atmosphere flowing, and fans of The Streets are excited for their first festival performance in over 8 years.
