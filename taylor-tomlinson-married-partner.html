Is Taylor Tomlinson Married? The Host of CBS Late Night Show

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Taylor Tomlinson’s relationship has been the talk of the town for some time. While she previously had a broken engagement with a stand-up comic partner, the question of being married or not from then onward is the question for many.

This article will first provide clarity on whether Taylor Tomlinson is married or not. Secondly, it will reveal facts about her previous partner, net worth, and her most recent association with the CBS family. 

American stand-up comedian Taylor Tomlinson is popular for her two Netflix stand-up specials, Quarter-Life Crisis (2020), and Look At You (2022). Her most recent work as a host is the CBS late-night program, After Midnight from 1 November 2023. 

Is Taylor Tomlinson Married?

The 29-year-old is not married but once engaged. As per The Things, the artist talked about her previous relationship during the March 2020 appearance on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon. Taylor spoke of having an amazing year of getting engaged and sarcastically citing it as not working out. 

Shedding light on their relationship, it was with fellow comedian Sam Morril from March 2020 to February 2022 per Wiki. It was in the same month when the early rise of COVID-19 spiked up and when the former used to post short clips together during quarantine. Moreover, the pair used to continue sharing footage of their life during lockdown which can eventually be a web series. 

There were also many rumors and speculations of the two dating even after the failed engagement. It was during the August 2021 episode of Late Night with Seth Meyers when the artist revealed her boyfriend was living in New York while raising in Manhattan. The narration suggested her former fiance was still part of her life. While she did not disclose the likely name and profession, it is still a mystery for many to know his actual identity.

Details About Her Former Partner

Taylor’s former partner, Sam Morril is 37 years of age, born on 29 August 1986. He grew up in New York City. Talking about his family, he was raised by parents Marilyn Goldberg and Charles Morril. While his mother is a painter, his step-father Charles is a publishing industry attorney. 

The artist went to Browning School in Manhattan and later chose Tulane University in New Orleans to complete his education. Some of his work credits citing comedy specials are Positive Influence (2018), I Got This (2020), Up On the Roof (2020), and Same Time Tomorrow (2022) to name a few,

Besides that, he has been part of the films like Joker (2019) and Full Capacity (2021) including a 2020 web series, Laugh Aid. Some of his TV work credits are Billions (2019), The Late Late Show with James Corden (2018), Jeff Ross Presents Roast Battle (2016), Last Comic Standing (2015), and Adam DeVine’s House Party (2014) to name a few.

Taylor Tomlinson Work Credits: Net Worth & CBS

As per Pinterest, the 29-year-old net worth is around $2 million as a stand-up comic since 2010. She began her career at the age of 16, performing in church basements, coffee shops, and school venues. Likewise, she became a top 10 finalist on the ninth season of NBC’s Last Comic Standing in 2015. In 2017, she developed a sitcom for ABC and three years later performed a 15-minute set on an episode of the Netflix stand-up series The Comedy Lineup in 2018. 

In March 2020, her first Netflix stand-up special Quarter-Life premiered on Netflix. A year later, she commended her podcast, Sad in the City, and was part of the Forbes 30 Under 30 in late 2021. Moreover, in March 2022, her second Netflix stand-up special Look At You premiered. 


The stand-up comic is the new host of the CBS late-night program, After Midnight from 1 November 2023. As per Variety, she’s the host of the CBS new late-night franchise produced by Stephen Colbert. 

Taylor Tomlinson Bio & Family: Wiki, Family & Education

The artist was born Taylor Elyse Tomlinson, age 29 on 4 November 1993 in Orange County, California. His family comprises his parents Eric Tomlinson and Angela Tomlinson. She is the eldest in the family and has three siblings named Drea, Page, and Brinn Tomlinson. When she was at the age of eight, her mother passed away from Cancer. Their father remarried, a year later. 

Taylor and her three siblings were raised in a devout Christian family.
