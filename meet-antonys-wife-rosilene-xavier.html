Meet Antonys wife Rosilene Xavier

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Antony Matheus is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a winger for Premier League club Manchester United and the Brazil national team.

Antony was raised in the remote favela of Inferninho after being born in Osasco, So Paulo, Brazil. He combined his love of futsal with street football while growing up, playing wherever he could on the streets or in his home. He also frequently accompanied his aunt to So Paulo matches. He has Portuguese ancestry.

Ajax announced on August 30, 2022, that they and Manchester United had reached a transfer agreement for Antony.

Two days later, Antony agreed to a five-year deal with Ajax for a transfer fee of €95 million (£82 million), plus an additional €5 million (£4.27 million) in add-ons.

This was the biggest transfer in Eredivisie history and the third-highest fee ever paid by the club, behind only Paul Pogba and Romelu Lukaku.

He was given the number 21 shirt that had been worn by Edinson Cavani, who had just left the club, upon his arrival.

On October 7, 2021, Antony made his debut for the Brazil national football team against Venezuela in a World Cup qualifying match.

He entered the game as a replacement in the 77th minute, and he scored a goal in extra time to give Brazil a 3-1 victory. He was added to the team for the 2022 FIFA World Cup on November 7, 2022.

Meet Antony’s wife Rosilene Xavier

Anthony is married to Rosilene Xavier, his longtime girlfriend. From an early age, Antony and Rosilene have been in love. When Antony was still a member of the Sao Paulo youth team, they first started dating.

When exactly Antony and Rosilene got married is unknown. Only their closest friends and family members were present at their intimate wedding ceremony.

Rosilene Xavier was very famous on Instagram before she decided to deactivate her account. She had over 80K followers. We are yet to unravel the reason behind her decision to delete her account and stay off the radar.
