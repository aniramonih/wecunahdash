Who is Simone Clarke?

Simone Clarke is a well-known Jamaican communication specialist. She has been in the news because her ex-husband, Simone Clarke, passed away recently.

She is from Kingston, Jamaica. After completing her schooling, she attended the University of the West Indies, Mona, where she first earned a Bachelor of Arts in Communication and Media Studies and later earned a master’s degree in communications.

Born Simon Nichola Clarke, Simon Clarke-Cooper was born to parents who hail from rural Jamaica and Arif Cooper’s ex-wife has an elder sister and two younger brothers – they grew up together. The media personality’s dad was a pastor, and her mom was a missionary.

She was married to well-known DJ Arif Cooper. They both have an 8 year old daughter named Aaryn. However, they both got divorced for unknown reasons. While her DOB isn’t known, she is believed to be aged between 35-35 years.

Her career

Simone Clarke started working at Television Jamaica after graduating, where she currently hosts the morning program Smile Jamaica. She has also worked as a Public Relations coordinator for Hilton Hotels and Resorts. Later, she joined GraceKennedy Ltd. as corporate communications manager.

Apart from this, she also held several positions at the RJR Communications Group and AVP, Group Corporate Communication.

She presently serves as the CEO of SimSpeak Communication, a business that gives both private individuals and corporate entities access to corporate communication consulting, voice and speech training, media coaching, broadcast training, and public speaking presentations.
