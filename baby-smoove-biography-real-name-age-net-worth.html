Early Life

Baby Smoove was born on December 3, 1990 in Detroit, Michigan, United States.

Career

Smoove began his music career under the name Bandrew Smoove before he later changed it to Baby Smoove in 2016.

He came through with his first single “Supersonic” alongside Rell, in September 2012. It was followed up with another song titled “Closer to my dreams”.

He eventually came to limelight in 2015 with his hit single “Never Stop Cashin”.

Ever since he rose to fame, he has released lots of songs such as; BAPE, Mr Perfect, Absurd and so on.

He released his first album “Riding with the Goat” on October 28, 2017 and his second album “Why So Serious” on April 13, 2018 which was followed up the same year with the album “I’m Still Serious”. He also got featured on Lil Yatchy single “Don’t Even Bother”.

Smoove released 4 albums in 2019 which are Flawless, Mr Perfect, Purple Heart and Baby and in 2020, he released 3 more which are; Practice, I’m still perfect and Hardwood classic.
