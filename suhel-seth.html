Suhel Seth Wiki

Suhel Seth is an Author, Actor, Speaker, Brand Manager, and marketing guru. He was born on 1963 in Kolkata, India. He is a managing partner of consultancy firm Counselage India, founded by him in June 2002. Suhel also worked at advertising agencies Response, Ogilvy & Mather and Equus. Other than this he also worked in movies. He made his movie debut with the film Rog in 2005. After this, he worked in movies like Guzaarish, Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara and Calendar Girls in 2015. 

Suhel is also an Author and has written two books: one ‘In Your Face’ a collection of his columns and the other, Kalighat to Calcutta in co-authorship with Khushwant Singh and R K Laxman. Suhel did his schooling from St. Joseph’s College, Nainital, and La Martiniere, Calcutta, He did his college studies from Jadavpur University. Suhel Seeth has the net worth of approx $3,000,000.
