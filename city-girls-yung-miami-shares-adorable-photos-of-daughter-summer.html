CITY GIRLS YUNG MIAMI SHARES ADORABLE PHOTOS OF DAUGHTER SUMMER

Publish date: 2024-08-23

City Girls group member Yung Miami, real name Caresha Brownlee, has a beautiful baby girl. The rapper shared pictures of her infant with her Instagram fans earlier this week.

One candid of Yung Miami’s daughter, whose name is Summer, shows her giving the camera full attention while sitting on her mom’s lap. Summer smiles in a different shot, which is something that Yung Miami’s social media fans absolutely loved to see.

“Such a pretty baby,” one fan said. “Just beautiful,” another Instagram follower agreed.

Yung Miami gave birth to her baby girl last October. The celebrity mom shared a picture of her newborn daughter resting next to her father, Southside, in the hospital to spread the word about Summer’s arrival.

“I’m convinced I have to be Chinese or Asian,” Yung Miami said when reminiscing about her daughter’s physical appeal. “My baby is everything,” the celebrity mom gushed.

Yung Miami shared the news of her expectancy with fans last Summer. The rapper was at first reluctant to go public with the news of her family’s growth for fear of what her pregnancy would mean for her budding career. Yung Miami, however, ultimately determined that her journey to motherhood was nothing short of a gift and, thus, decided to come out with the announcement regardless of the consequences.

The rapper was pleasantly surprised to learn that the public was welcoming of her pregnancy. “I received a lot of love,” Yung Miami said of the public’s response to her expectancy during a recent interview. “Everyone was very supportive of me,” she added. “I feel honored and blessed.”

Summer is Yung Miami’s second child. Stay tuned for more celebrity baby news!
