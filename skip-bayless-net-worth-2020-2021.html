Career

Bayless started working as a columnist in The Miami Herald, but his career actually took off when he got the call from Los Angeles Times in 1976. He had worked in radio stations like KLIF and KTCK. Bayless also worked as a show host in ESPN Radio. He joined ESPN network as a sports commentator and started hosting the show The Sports Reporters. Bayless also started working in ESPN.com as a sports columnist. Later he joined Fox Sports channel and began hosting the sports talk shows. One of his popular shows is First Take and Sports Center. He also acted in some films in cameo roles.
