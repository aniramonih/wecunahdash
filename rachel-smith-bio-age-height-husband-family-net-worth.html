Rachel Smith Biography

Rachel Smith is an American Actress, model, TV host, and beauty pageant titleholder who won the 2007 Miss USA. Previously, she had competed in the Miss Teen USA 2002 pageant. In addition, she represented the United States at the 2007 Miss Universe where she was ranked the 4th runner-up.

Rachel was formerly the co-host of ABC’s nationally syndicated entertainment show, OnTheRedCarpet. Later on, she worked as a reporter for E! Nevertheless worked as a correspondent on the Saturday Telecast of Good Morning America. At the moment, she serves as the co-host of Amazin’s Style Code.

In 1993, Smith was featured in the Miniature Miss Divison at the Cinderella International Pageant. She began her TV Career in Pageant Place a Donald Trump’s MTV reality show. She worked long with the Miss Universe 2007, former Miss USA 2006, Miss Teen USA 2006, and Miss Teen USA 2007.

Moreover, Rachel appeared on a special Girls of Summer week on an episode called Last Beauty Standing in August 2010. The show aired on NBC’s Minute to Win It. Nevertheless, she also worked as a guest correspondent for ET interviewing famous people like Lionel Richie, Katy Perry, Dua Lipa, Kim Kardashian, and Rihanna among others.

Smith attended Clarksville Academy and later on in Davidson Academy for High School. Afterward, she joined Belmont University in Nashville, Tennessee graduating with a Bachelor of Science in Journalism. She received a full-tuition scholarship to attend Belmont, where she interned with Oprah Winfrey’s company, Harpo Productions, Chicago-based.

Smith was also announced and chosen by Winfrey to volunteer for a month at her Leadership Academy for Girls. She was also on the Dean’s List and a member of Phi Eta Sigma Honor Society. Rachel Smith also appeared in the movie Last Resort in 2011.
