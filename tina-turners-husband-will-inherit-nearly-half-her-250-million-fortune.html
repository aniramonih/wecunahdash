Tina Turner's husband Erwin Bach, 67, is seen for first time since her death

Publish date: 2024-08-27

Tina Turner’s husband Erwin Bach has been spotted for the first since her death last week. 

The 67-year-old made his first appearance since the tragic death of the music icon – who passed away at the age of 83 in Küsnacht near Zurich following a long, unspecified illness. She had previously battled intestinal cancer and suffered with high blood pressure for over four decades.

Now, just over a week later, Erwin was pictured outside of their $76,000,000 home placing flowers at his late wife’s memorial, which is located in Küsnacht, Switzerland. 

He left red roses at the site and was seen organizing the other gifts that poured in from fans and friends of the legendary singer.
