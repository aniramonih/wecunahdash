Wikipedia Biography / Profile Background

Introduction :

  • Philip Schneider is an American social venture entrepreneur, producer, and writer.
  • He has emerged into the spotlight as the husband of acclaimed Hollywood actress Hilary Swank, but still enjoys keeping most of his life private.

Personal Life, Education and Career :

  • Philip Schneider was born in 1973 in California to father Phil Schneider.
  • He graduated with a business degree from the University of Texas in Austin.
  • Philip is currently working as an executive producer for J. Walter Thompson, a marketing communications company based in New York City.
  • He has written and produced four short films between 2013 and 2014: TSA America: Just Relax; Don’t Miss the Cup; TSA America: Suspicious Bulges; and TSA America: Yeah, But Is It Ticking?
  • Philip has also worked for Saatchi & Saatchi and Draftfcb.

Start of a Relationship :

  • Philip married lauded Hollywood actress Hilary Swank on August 18, 2018. Before this, Swank was married to Chad Lowe from 1997–2007.
  • The two were introduced in 2015 on a blind date set up by Philip’s friend, actor Misha Collins’ wife. Surprisingly, the first date went on for more than twelve hours.
  • The couple made their debut in front of the cameras when they went skiing together at the Corviglia Snowpark in St. Moritz, Switzerland.
  • Philip later proposed to Hilary in Colorado, which was witnessed by the actress’s two dogs.

The Wedding Ceremony :

  • Swank and Philip’s wedding took place on the Santa Lucia Preserve in Carmel, California, a stunning nature reserve featuring a redwood grove that was a fitting location for two nature lovers.
  • The ceremony was attended by many famous faces, such as Mariska Hargitay, was Hilary’s maid of honour. Philip’s friend actor Misha Collins wrote a poem for the couple that was read out during the wedding reception.
  • Philip’s best man was his father, Phil.
  • Philip performed a tap-dancing routine choreographed by Chloe Arnold with Hilary for the wedding reception to the tune of singer Moby’s song ‘In My Heart.’ The tapping shoes Philip wore were designed by the very trendy shoe designer Christian Louboutin & the suit he wore during the dance routine was designed by Dior.
  • The vows during the wedding ceremony were set to the romantic tones of ‘Hoppípolla,’ a song by Icelandic band Sigur Rós.
  • The service was hosted by Reverend Michael Beckwith, one of the couple’s friends.
