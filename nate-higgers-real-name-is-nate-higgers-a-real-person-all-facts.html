Nate Higgers Real Name: Is Nate Higgers a Real Person? All Facts

Publish date: 2024-08-24

Nate Higgers is not a real person, but rather a name associated with the acting CEO of Google. Originally, it was just two words that had been cleverly disguised to avoid offending anyone. The individual going by the moniker Nate Higgers is anonymous and appears to be the acting CEO of the IT company.

An online joke regarding Google’s allegedly racially prejudiced company culture against Black individuals gave rise to the name Nate Higgers. If you change the initial letters of the name, it will become clearer how Nate Higgers came to be Google’s acting CEO1. The name was already in use prior to its use as the name of Google’s acting CEO.

When someone refers to you as Nate Higgers, they are effectively accusing you of racism and hatred toward Black people.

When some Twitter and Reddit users began making jokes about Nate Higger taking over as Google’s acting CEO, the tech company’s acting CEO became well-known online. Naturally, this piqued people’s interest, and many began searching for “acting CEO of Google” on Google to see if Sundar Pichai had resigned as the company’s CEO. A Linkedin profile with the name Nate Higgers12 was found through the searches. Naturally, Google does not have an acting CEO, and the name Nate Higgers is fictitious. The Linkedin profile and name were intended to be a prank, criticizing Google for their perceived racially biased corporate culture against people of color
