Biography

Jessica Yu-Li Henwick (born August 30, 1992) is a talented and versatile English actress, writer, and director. From her early days in captivating BBC series like Spirit Warriors (2010) and Silk (2010) to her memorable roles in popular franchises, Henwick has solidified her presence in the entertainment industry.

One of her breakthrough roles came in dragons and power struggles, as she portrayed the fierce and enigmatic Nymeria Sand in HBO’s acclaimed series Game of Thrones (2015–2017). Her portrayal of the formidable warrior showcased her range and garnered admiration from viewers worldwide.

Venturing into the faraway galaxy, Jessica Henwick made her mark in Star Wars: The Force Awakens (2015) as the intriguing Testor. Her performance captured the imagination of audiences, adding depth to the epic space opera.

Jessica Henwick‘s talents also extended to the superhero sphere, with her portrayal of the resilient Colleen Wing in Marvel’s Iron Fist series (2017–2018). Through her compelling portrayal, she brought the complex character to life, earning acclaim from fans of the genre.

Beyond television, Jessica Henwick has ventured into film, gracing the screen in notable works such as Love and Monsters (2020) and the highly anticipated The Matrix Resurrections (2021). Her ability to immerse herself in diverse roles showcases her versatility as an actress.

Demonstrating her creative prowess, Jessica Henwick delved into directing and writing with her short film Bus Girl (2022), which received critical acclaim and earned her a prestigious British Academy Film Award nomination. This accomplishment highlights her ambition and willingness to explore new artistic avenues.
