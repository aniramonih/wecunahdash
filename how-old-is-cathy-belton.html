How Old Is Cathy Belton? Meet Actor Who Plays Liz From Nocebo

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Find out “How Old Is Cathy Belton?” Cathy Belton, an Irish actress, age 52, plays the role of Liz in her upcoming movie ‘Nocebo.’

The folk horror directed by Lorcan Finnegan is set to make its initial release in Ireland on December 9, 2022. The folk horror is supposed to contain a home invasion twist.

For Cathy, this is not her first time being a part of something extraordinary. She has appeared on different stages, radio, films, and television productions.

She is best known for Philomena (2013), Red Rock (2015), and Hidden Assets (2021).

1. Cathy Belton Started Her Career At The Age Of 23

Cathy Belton is 52 years old. She was born on January 17, 1970.

As a child, she grew up in her hometown, Renmore, Galway, Ireland. When she turned 17, she moved from her hometown to Dublin to attend Trinity College Dublin.

When the independent interviewed her, she talked about what she was like as a kid. She said she was shy as a kid but had a wild imagination, constantly dreaming up new worlds while spending time in her granny’s house.

Over the years, she overcame her shy nature through her profession. The talented and much-loved actress has contributed to the movie industry and theatre equally and continues to work even now when she is in her fifties.

2. Cathy Belton Dated Her Husband, Brian Roe, For Four Years Before Getting Married

Cathy Belton married her husband, Brian Roe, in the summer of 2018. Roe is a solicitor based in Dublin, Ireland.

Cathay and Brian were first introduced to each other by some ‘matchmaking’ friends, according to Sunday Independent Living magazine. She was asked if she would meet him, but Roe didn’t know about her until they met.

Following their first meeting, they met again at a common friend’s wedding, where he asked if she would date him. She agreed, seeing they are married now.

She felt just right to be with him and considered him beautiful. She thinks her friends did a perfect job by matching them.

The “Hidden Assets” star and her boyfriend of four years exchanged vows in a small ceremony in the presence of their family, close friends, and a few colleagues. They had a destination wedding in rural France over the weekend. She wore a stunning Peter O’Brien wedding dress.

Belton is currently staying at home with her husband, Brian Roe. The couple lives near the Grand Canal Harbour in Dublin.

3. She Shared A Very Special Bond With Her Late Father

Growing up in Galway, she had a very close relationship with her parents and family.

They were there whenever she needed them and supported her through all her decisions.

Usually, Cathay does not talk much about her family. In 2012, she spoke of her father with the Irish Examiner, where she shared what her father used to tell her when she was younger.

He always told her to treat people how she would want to be treated, and now, it is the best advice she has ever gotten for her.

She thinks that it is so essential, particularly in her line of profession. As acting can be challenging, it helps when people can be kind to each other.

Her father passed away in 2006 after suffering from cancer for a long time. Losing her father was one of the biggest challenges she had to face.

4. Belton Was Insecure Early In Her Career And Almost Quit Acting

Cathy Belton’s first part was in a play by Walter Macken called “Home is the Hero” in the Island Theatre Company.

Subsequently, sid did the Tempest and the Hamlet, but the work she got gradually stopped, and she started to grow insecure. She wondered whether she was cut out for that kind of insecurity, so she opted to follow a Plan B.

The actress had always wanted to be a national school teacher. Her parents were also supportive of her decision. She almost got into teaching permanently.

But eventually, she was convinced by Mike Diskin, who ran the Town Hall Theatre in Galway. While on tour with his production, she auditioned for Glenroe and got the part.

She was Lucy Reily, the vet in Glenroe, for two years. She felt like she was given Glenroe for security, which was a sign for her. She felt like she couldn’t run away from what was meant for her and eventually got back to acting.

5. She Has A Younger Sibling, Orina Belton

Cathy is the eldest child of the Belton family but not a single child. She has a younger sister named Orina Belton.

The Belton sisters grew up together in their hometown, Galway. For Cathy, being an older sister meant being a pioneer, and she also had to chip down boundaries for her younger sister, Orina.

Orina is not in show business like her sister. She has a BSc. in biochemistry and a Ph.D. in Clinical Pharmacology. She is currently the head of the pharmacology department and also a professor at University College Dublin.

She is active on Twitter under the username @OBelton and can also be found on LinkedIn.

Although Cathy’s younger sister is not seen under the spotlight with her very often, the sisters are close to each other and spend time with each other.
