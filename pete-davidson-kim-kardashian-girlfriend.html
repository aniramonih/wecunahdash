Pete Davidson Made Things Official With Kim Kardashian, His Girlfriend

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Pete Davidson made things super official with Kim Kardashian by putting a title on their relationship. The Saturday Night Live star referred to the shapewear mogul as his "girlfriend" during a recent interview.

"I don't have Instagram or Twitter or any of that stuff. So, most of my daily life is getting into cars and showing up to a set," he explained to People. "Or, if I'm off, I just either hang with my friends or chill with my girlfriend inside."

He added that his rise to fame has had almost "zero" impact on his daily life. "Once in a while, someone will scream something at you, or it might be hard to get Dunkin' Donuts. But other than that, it's pretty fine. It's not awful. It could be way worse."

The two have been linked since last October following Kim's SNL hosting debut. Since then, the two have been spotted out on dates in Pete's native Staten Island, Palm Springs, Calif., and on a romantic rendezvous in the Bahamas last month.

As for what's next for the couple, Pete appears to have something up his sleeve for Valentine's Day. "I don't think I've ever had a Valentine's Day thing really. So this would be the first year that I'm thinking about Valentine's Day plans, I guess."

He continued, "It's a big day. It's another Super Bowl. Super Bowl for the ladies."
