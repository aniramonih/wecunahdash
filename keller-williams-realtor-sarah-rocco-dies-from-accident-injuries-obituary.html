Keller Williams Realtor Sarah Rocco dies from accident injuries: Obituary

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Jacksonville, Florida, mourns the untimely loss of Sarah Rocco, a revered realtor with Keller Williams, who tragically succumbed to injuries sustained in a motor vehicle collision involving a Tesla on Monday, December 11. The collision left Rocco severely injured, and despite efforts, she passed away while surrounded by her family after being admitted to the Intensive Care Unit (ICU).

Hailing from the unpretentious state of Montana, Sarah Rocco was not just a realtor but the proprietor and chief executive officer of The Rocco Group at Keller Williams Atlantic Partners Southside in Jacksonville. A jubilant mother of three extraordinary children, Rocco dedicated her life to serving as a paradigm of what is achievable. Her mission extended beyond the realms of real estate, as she guided and motivated individuals to attain extraordinary accomplishments and lead remarkable lives.

Rocco’s profound understanding of the real estate sector, fueled by her passion for the subject, made her an invaluable asset accessible to every client. Her commitment to providing value and cultivating relationships consistently yielded favorable outcomes, showcasing her dedication to excellence. As the eloquent and dynamic professional at the helm of The Rocco Group, she led with integrity, inspiring her team and encouraging others in the pursuit of personal objectives.

Establishing The Rocco Group in 2017, Sarah Rocco dedicated countless hours of labor and unwavering dedication, propelling the organization to rank in the top 0.1% of Jacksonville Realtors. The company swiftly became one of the most rapidly expanding real estate firms in the nation under her guidance.

Beyond her local impact, Sarah Rocco served as a featured adviser at the University of Florida: Warrington Business Sales Seminars and gained national recognition as a trainer and speaker. Balancing roles as an entrepreneur, motivational speaker, and agent, she aspired to influence and be influenced by her surroundings, aiming to create a lasting positive impact for future generations.

The family of Sarah Rocco will release details regarding her obituary and funeral plans at a later date. The community, colleagues, and clients are invited to join in remembering and celebrating the life of a remarkable individual who made indelible contributions to both the real estate industry and the lives she touched.
