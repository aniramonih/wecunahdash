Brittany &amp; Patrick Mahomes Son Bronze Side-Eyes Camera: Photo

Publish date: 2024-08-05

Bronze Mahomes is giving Kaavia Union-Wade a serious run for her money in the Shady Baby department.

On Sunday, the 1-year-old accompanied his mom, Brittany Mahomes, to watch Kansas City Chiefs papa Patrick Mahomes play football. While attending an NFL game is a super exciting occasion for many people, Bronze, it would appear, is not one of them.

The mom of two shared a hilarious snapshot of herself holding her son on the sidelines, and the baby boy was unimpressed to the fullest extent. In the photo, Brittany squatted while resting Bronze on her knee with his body facing hers. The 1-year-old wasn’t looking at his mom though — he was craned around looking at the camera with the most savage side-eye we’ve ever seen, and we can’t help but cackle.
