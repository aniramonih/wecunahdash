    Wells Adams Facts

  • He started working as a radio host in Nashville in 2007 and has worked for radio stations like iHeartRadio stations Alt 98.3 and 105.9 The Rock.
  • He also co-hosts the podcast, Your Favorite Thing, along with Miley Cyrus‘ older sister, Brandi Cyrus, on which they discuss various topics such as music and politics.
  • He was encouraged by his older brother to audition for The Bachelorette after he became single. Wells has said in an interview that his brother had also auditioned for the show 6 years before him but he bailed out after he got into a relationship.
  • Wells has claimed that he did not take the audition very seriously and saw it as one of the stories that he could tell in the future that how he auditioned for a reality TV show and got rejected.

  • He is not a pizza lover.
