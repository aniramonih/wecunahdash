Wisin and wife mourn loss of their 1-month-old daughter Victoria

Publish date: 2024-08-26

Wisin has announced the heartbreaking news that his newborn daughter Victoria has passed away.

The 37-year-old Puerto Rican star shared a photo of his wife Yomaira Ortiz Feliciano with their one-month-old baby girl to Instagram on Friday to tell his fans the sad news.

'My princess just died' the rapper put in the caption of the black and white photo showing Yomaira tending to the tiny tot in an incubator.

'I will see you again in heaven': Puerto Rican musician Wisin made the heartbreaking announcement on Friday that he and his wife have lost their one-month-old daughter Victoria

'Thank you, my God, for one month of life, for one month of Victoria. God gives, and God takes away, blessed be the name of the Lord. Princess Victoria, I will see you again in heaven.' he wrote.

'What immense pain I have, God, please help me. I want to use this moment to thank the millions of people that were praying for our family and our Victoria. Thank you because in all of your gestures and words that you had with us we were able to see God each time,'

Victoria was born with genetic disorder Trisomy 13 which causes a number of birth defects including severe intellectual disability as well as health problems involving nearly every organ system.
