Early Life & Biography

Larissa de Macedo Machado was born on 30th March 1993 in Rio de Janeiro in Brazil. Her parents Mauro Machado and Miriam Macedo, are both Brazilian natives and live in Rio de Janeiro. In her family. Her artistic producer is her brother Renan Machado who she grew up with as both were raised by their mother.

Anitta took up singing while she was eight years old when she joined her church choir in Santa Luzia. This was after her grandparents convinced her to do so. By the age of eleven, she was so determined to learn the English language that she started paying for her classes from her allowances. She later took dancing lessons from her mother’s dance teacher.

Besides Music and dancing, Anitta joined a technical school where she graduated at the age of sixteen with an administration course. After taking music as her career, she took Anitta as her stage whose inspiration came from the 2001 TV series in Brazil called Presenca de Anita. Besides Portuguese, which is Brazil’s native language, she is fluent in English and Spanish.

Career

Anitta’s professional career started when producer Renato Azevedo discovered her after watching some of her music videos. After doing well during her performance tests, she was signed by Furacao 2000 and immediately released her first single “Eu Vou Ficar.” In 2012, she sang Claudia Leitte’s song “Exttravasa” on television show Cante se Puder.

In 2013, her song Meiga e Abusada brought so much fame to her that American multinational label signed her. Anitta recorded her first live album on 15th February 2014 in Rio de Janeiro in the event that was attended by around 10,000 people.

In 2015, she released her third album, Bang, which in Brazil was rated as the most anticipated albums. More than 40,000 copies of the album were sold in the first week of its release.  By May 2016, the album had already sold more than 300,000 copies, which surpassed her first album sales in a very big margin. Her Bang single in the album was watched internationally, and by 2020 May, the song has more than 3900 million views on YouTube.

Awards & Achievements

With her career in music starting with a bang, she has been nominated and won several awards on the way. In 2014, she won Best Brazilian Act award with her first album. She later won the same award from 2015 to 2018, with her music being rated the best in Brazil. Other awards she has won include Premio Jovem Brasileiro, Premio Multishow, Breakdown Awards, ERES Mexico Awards several times, and many more.
