Rhona Mitra is a British actor, model, singer, and songwriter. She is known for having been cast in roles like Claire Radcliff in the ABC supernatural crime drama series The Gates, Dr. Rachel Scott in the TNT action-drama series The Last Ship, Holly Marie Beggins in the teen and family drama series Party of Five, Tara Wilson in both The Practice as well as in Boston Legal, Mercy Graves in The CW superhero series Supergirl, Maj. Rachel Dalton in the action-adventure spy drama series Strike Back, Dr. Alejandra Ollie Klein in the medical drama series Gideon’s Crossing, and Kit McGraw in the FX medical drama series Nip/Tuck. Rhona Mitra has also been a part of movies such as Monk Dawson, A Kid in Aladdin’s Palace, Hollow Man, Ali G Indahouse, Sweet Home Alabama, The Life of David Gale, Stuck on You, Stolen, Skinwalkers, Highwaymen, The Number 23, Shooter, Underworld: Rise of the Lycans, Separation City, Reuniting the Rubins, Lust for Glorious, The Loft, The Fight, Archive, Skylines, Crisis Point, and Game Over, Man!.
