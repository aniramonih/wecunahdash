Storyline and Plot

The storyline of Helck is a mix of comedy, action, fantasy, and drama. It explores the themes of friendship, love, war, peace, and destiny. The main characters are Helck, a human hero who claims to hate humanity, and Vamirio, a demon princess who distrusts him. They are joined by other characters, such as Piwi, a bird-like creature who is Helck’s loyal companion, Hyura, a demon warrior who is Vamirio’s friend, Azudora, a dragon-like demon who is one of the Four Heavenly Kings, and Isuta, a human girl who is Helck’s childhood friend.

The story begins with the death of the Demon King, who was killed by a human hero. The demons decide to hold a tournament to choose the next Demon King, and Helck, who is surprisingly strong, joins the tournament. Vamirio, who is one of the judges, suspects that Helck has ulterior motives and tries to expose him. However, she gradually learns that Helck is not a bad person, but rather a kind and gentle soul who cares for everyone. She also discovers that Helck has a tragic past, as he lost his family and friends in a war between humans and demons.

As the story progresses, Helck and Vamirio face various enemies and challenges, such as the Demon Empire, a faction of demons who want to conquer the world, the Hero Association, a group of humans who want to exterminate the demons, and the Ancient King, a mysterious being who is behind the disappearance of humans from various towns.
