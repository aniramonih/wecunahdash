Sofia Vergara Celebrates Birthday With Joe Manganiello T-Shirt, Fiance

Publish date: 2024-08-17

If you’ve got it, flaunt it! Sofia Vergara rang in her 43rd birthday in style on Friday, July 10, with an entourage of family, friends, and her fiancé, Magic Mike star Joe Manganiello.

The Modern Family actress excitedly shared several snapshots from the evening on Instagram, but one in particular stood out: Vergara sipping from a chalice while wearing a white T-shirt sporting none other than Manganiello’s face. But more than that, however, was the fact that Manganiello’s muscular upper torso was Photoshopped onto a horse’s body, making him a super hot centaur.

“Happy verde,” she captioned the snap, in which she also dons a sparkly tiara and a pink sash with the words “Birthday Princess” written across it.

In other photos from the night, Vergara posed with a tableful of close friends and family — with her and Manganiello, 38, posing happily in the back.

Another particularly cute photo shows the Hollywood hunk planting a kiss on Vergara’s head as the pair enjoy the twilight together.

Earlier this year, the pair, who began dating in July 2014, celebrated Vergara’s son Manolo Gonzalez’s graduation from Emerson College together in Boston.
