BIOGRAPHY
Thomas Kent Carter, known simply as TK Carter , is an American actor and comedian, born December 18, 1956, in New York. Outgoing and humorous since he was a child, TK used to perform his comedy routines at his school when he was 12 years old. Still a teenager, he landed a frequent routine at the Comedy Store in Los Angeles and years later was part of the Los Angeles Actors Theater. With just over 60 credits in film and television, Carter has appeared in films such as The Thing (1982), Ski Patrol (1990), My Favorite Martian (1999), Domino (2005) and in series such as Punky Brewster (1985- 1986), Good Morning, Miss Bliss (1989), The Corner (2000) and The Bobby Brown Story (2018). TK Carter has secured a role in Gavin O’Connor’s upcoming film, The Way Back , due out in 2020.
