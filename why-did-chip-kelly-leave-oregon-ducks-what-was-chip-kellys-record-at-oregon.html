Why did Chip Kelly leave Oregon Ducks? What was Chip Kellys record at Oregon?

Publish date: 2024-08-05

Chip Kelly is an American football coach and former player who formerly served as the head coach of the Philadelphia Eagles and the San Francisco 49ers of the National Football League.

Chip Kelly currently serves as the head coach of the UCLA Bruins of the University of California, Los Angeles. Chip Kelly formerly played college football as a defensive back for the New Hampshire Wildcats football team of the University of New Hampshire.

Why did Chip Kelly leave Oregon Ducks?

Chip Kelly left the Oregon Ducks as he was ready for a new challenge and also in order to improve the Philadelphia Eagles in order for them to become championship contenders.

Chip Kelly was very hopeful about shaping the gameplay of the Philadelphia Eagles of the National Football League.

What was Chip Kelly’s record at Oregon?

Chip Kelly’s win-loss record at the Oregon Ducks of the University of Oregon was 46-7.
