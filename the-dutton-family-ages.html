What Are The Ages Of The Dutton Family Yellowstone?

Publish date: 2024-08-15

The lead of the 1883 series James Dutton was the original patriarch of the Dutton family. He was also the first one to claim the family's home in Montana.

Jacob Dutton is one of the leading men in 1923 and the brother to James who took over caring for the Yellowstone homestead in the early 1900s.

Similarly, James ad Jacob's sister Claire was shown in 1883. Her family ended as her children and husband were killed and she chose to commit suicide.

Elsa, the eldest child of James and Margaret, provided the narration for the 1883 tale before she passed away in the final season.

John Dutton I is the oldest son of James and Margaret. He was seen in 1883 as a child and adult in the 1923 series.

The third child of James and Margaret Dutton is Spencer Dutton. He played the main character in the 1923 series.

Jack Dutton is the son of Emma and John Dutton I. He is seen in 1923, he later married Elizabeth Strafford in 1923.

John Dutton II is said to be the son of Spencer and Alex. Some also believe that he was the son of Jack and Elizabeth.

He was seen as an old man in flashback episodes of Yellowstone and also served once as the Dutton family patriarch.

John Dutton III is the current owner of the massive Yellowstone ranch. He is powerful and rebellious in nature.

In addition to serving as governor of Montana, he has deep ties to his family, his principles, and his traditions. His wife Evelyn passed away after giving birth to four kids.

Lee Dutton is the eldest child of John Dutton III. He was always meant to carry the ranch legacy but he passed away in the first season.

His only daughter Beth is one of the powerful characters of the series. She is a bold, fierce, and strong-minded woman married to Rip Wheeler.

Kayce Dutton is the youngest son of the family, married to Monica Long. Similarly, Jamie is the adopted child of John Dutton III.

Kayce and Monica shared one son named Tate Dutton, and Jamaie and his wife Christina also have one son named Jamie Dutton II.

How Does The Dutton Family Tree Work?

The Dutton family tree works by passage through seven generations. Tate and Jamie II are the seventh generations of the Dutton family. 

Generation 1 includes Jacob and James Dutton, they were shown in both the 1883 and 1893 prequels of the Yellowstone.

James' brother Jacob takes charge of Yellowstone and turns it into an empire for the family.

Generation 2 includes Elsa, John, and Spencer Dutton. Elsa passed away on the Oregon Trail and Spencer was a veteran of World War I.

Spencer Dutton age is mid to late thirties, according to the Express. His story is still incomplete in the Yellowstone prequel series.

Jack Dutton and John Dutton II fall under Generation 4. Jack was seen in 1923 while John was part of Yellowstone.

The Generation Five are John Dutton III and Peter Dutton. Peter passed away early after his birth.

Finally, the sixth generation are Lee, Beth, Jamie, and Kayce Dutton. They are the major character of the Yellowstone series.
