While Mike makes waves with bogeys & birdies, his longtime girlfriend-turned-fiancee keeps herself occupied with TV projects & entrepreneurship.

Michelle Money, a reality TV personality & actress, won Season 1 of Bachelor in Paradise. Aside from that, she has expertise as a hairstylist & beauty educator.

Both of them have already gone through a failed marriage earlier.

The 2003 Masters Tournament winner is father of two daughters with his ex-wife Bricia Rodriguez. They were married for 21 years before they divorced in 2015.

And as for Michelle, she was in a marital relationship with Ryan Money & shares a daughter named Brielle Money with him.

Michelle Money: Soon-To-Be Wife Of Mike Weir!

Michelle Money, born on September 28, 1980, is an actress, producer, hairstylist &  entrepreneur.

She began her career at 14, starring in a music video for the Backstreet Boys.

Her on-screen credits include The Eleventh Hour (2008), Inhumane (2018), and By Any Means (2017).

However, she is best known for her appearances on reality TV shows, like The Bachelor, Bachelor in Paradise, and Bachelor Pad.

Michelle participated in the 15th season of The Bachelor, but unfortunately, she was eliminated in week 7. Later, she appeared in the 2nd season of Bachelor Pad and was eliminated in episode 7.

Michelle eventually went on to win the first season of ‘Bachelor in Paradise.’

Besides her acting and reality TV career, Money runs an online women’s golf brand, Fore All. 

Brief On Her Family!

She is one of six children to her parents, Scott Edward Cartwright and Marjorie Abegg.

Her father (b. April 12, 1957- d. August 4, 2011) died of colon cancer at age 54.

Scott, a graduate of Hillcrest High School, remained in a marital relationship with his wife Marjorie for 33 years until his death. They married on July 5, 1978.

Late Cartwright is survived by his wife & six children- that includes Michelle & her siblings: Ann Bangerter (Scott), Jeffrey Cartwright, Paul Cartwright, Douglas Cartwright, and Denise Cartwright.

Engaged With Mike Weir

Michelle Money is currently engaged with Mike Weir, a professional golfer from Canada. They started dating in 2016, a year after Mike divorced Bricia Rodriguez.

Michelle took to Instagram to publicly reveal their relationship in August of that year, posting a picture of Mike with the caption “mine” and a heart emoji.

The couple announced via Instagram on January 8, 2023, that they will soon be tying the knot after being in a relationship for almost seven years.

Previous Marriage Of Michelle Money

Michelle Money was previously in a marital relationship with Ryan Money, with whom she shares a daughter named Brielle.

Ryan Money was born in 1980 and graduated from Westminister College in 2003.

The two met in 2003 at a friend’s wedding and began dating shortly after.

At that time, Ryan was working for the HP company, and after a few years of relationship, the couple tied the knot in 2005.

However, their marriage ended in 2010 & their daughter’s custody went to Michelle by mutual consent.

Ryan has since remarried Ashley Money, and they have four children, including two daughters and two sons. He currently works as an advisor/investor for Corso.

Daughter Was Hospitalized After An Accident

With her ex-husband Ryan Money, Michelle is the mother of a daughter named Brielle Money, born on February 7, 2005.

In March 2020, a serious skateboarding accident occurred that caused her to suffer a fractured skull and other severe injuries, leading her to remain in a coma for two weeks.

She is currently a student at Corner Canyon High School.

Besides Brielle, the reality TV personality, was also a mother to a son named Preston, whom she gave birth to at the age of 15. But she made the difficult decision to place him for adoption, but she has recently reconnected with him.

However, the identity of Preston’s biological father is currently unknown.

Michelle Money’s Short-Lived Relationship

During her stint on Bachelor in Paradise, Michelle Money was in a relationship with Cody Sattler.

Their relationship was brief, as they parted ways after just six months of dating in 2015.

Cody Sattler participated in the 10th season of The Bachelorette, where he was eliminated in week 6. He then competed on the first season of Bachelor in Paradise, which he won alongside Michelle Money.

Mike Weir’s Ex-Wife & Fatherhood

Mike Weir met his former wife, Bricia Rodriguez, at Brigham Young University, where they were both attendees.

They began dating during Mike’s sophomore year and tied the knot in April 1994 after dating for five years.

Bricia, who played on the amateur tennis circuit, was born to Mexican immigrants and grew up in Los Angeles, California.

After getting married, the couple settled in Utah, and Bricia caddied for Mike for three years. They had two children during their 21-year marriage.

Unfortunately, in 2015, Mike and Bricia divorced, and the reason for their divorce remains unknown.

Two Daughters: Elle & Lilli Weir

Mike Weir has two daughters: Elle Weir, who was born on December 19, 1997, and Lily Weir, who was born on April 3, 2000.

Elle attended The Waterford School for high school and graduated from Pepperdine University in Sports Medicine.

Currently, she works as an Integrated Case Assistant at the ASPCA in New York.

Lily also attended The Waterford School and is currently studying at Colorado College.
