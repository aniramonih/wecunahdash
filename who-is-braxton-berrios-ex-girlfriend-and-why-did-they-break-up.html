Who is Braxton Berrios Ex Girlfriend and Why Did They Break Up?

Braxton Berrios, a professional football player currently with the Miami Dolphins, is recognized not only for his sports career but also for his personal life. Notably, his relationship with Sophia Culpo, a reality TV star and model, gained attention. Their two-year romance came to an end in March 2023 amid swirling rumors of infidelity and betrayal. In this blog post, we will delve into the background of Sophia Culpo, explore the reasons behind her split with Berrios, and uncover the aftermath of their separation.

Who is Sophia Culpo?

Sophia Culpo, a 26-year-old influencer and model, rose to prominence through TLC’s reality show, “The Culpo Sisters,” alongside her sisters Olivia and Aurora. Olivia, a former Miss Universe and Sports Illustrated Swimsuit model, and Aurora, a singer and songwriter, complete the trio. Sophia, a successful TikTok star with over 1.5 million followers, shares fashion, beauty, and lifestyle tips, as well as glimpses into her personal and family life.

Sophia and Berrios first crossed paths in 2021 and quickly became a public couple, documenting their travels, holidays, and celebrations on social media. Their relationship led them to cohabitate in New Jersey while Berrios played for the New York Jets. However, the apparent happiness took a downturn in 2023.

How and Why Did Sophia and Berrios Break Up?

While the precise details of Sophia and Berrios’ breakup remain unclear, signs point to issues of infidelity and dishonesty. Sophia publicly announced their split in a TikTok video in March 2023, referring to herself and her sister Aurora as “the single sisters” and using hashtags like #sosingle, #singlelife, and #breakuptiktok. In an Instagram Q&A session, she disclosed that their relationship ended poorly, with significant trust broken. Sophia felt betrayed, necessitating her move from their shared home to Los Angeles with friends.

Around the same time, rumors circulated that Berrios had cheated with another influencer, Alix Earle. Earle, known for her TikTok “Get Ready With Me” videos, hinted at a connection with Berrios in a March 2023 video. Although not mentioning Berrios by name, fans noticed his interaction, further fueling speculations.

What Happened After Sophia and Berrios’ Breakup?

The aftermath of Sophia and Berrios’ breakup unfolded dramatically on social media. Sophia received support and sympathy from fans, while her sisters, Olivia and Aurora, offered words of encouragement. Berrios faced backlash from Sophia’s supporters, and Sophia herself hinted at dissatisfaction through cryptic TikTok posts.

Berrios and Earle continued making public appearances together, adding fuel to romance rumors. They eventually confirmed their relationship in November 2023 with a kissing photo on Earle’s Instagram.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Braxton Berrios and Sophia Culpo ended their two-year relationship in March 2023 due to issues of cheating and betrayal. Berrios moved on with Alix Earle, confirming their relationship in November 2023, while Sophia focused on her career and family, navigating the aftermath of a highly publicized split.
