Pam Smith top movies, TV shows and awards

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Pam Smith is an actress who came to the limelight by being featured in the movies such as What to Expect When You’re Expecting (2012), Allegiant (2016), and Prisoners (2013).

Allegiant

Allegiant, a 2016 American dystopian science fiction action film written by Bill Collage, Adam Cooper, and Noah Oppenheim and directed by Robert Schwentke, is the third and final book in the Divergent series.

It was meant to be the first of two movies based on the final book in Veronica Roth’s Divergent trilogy (the second cinematic part was to be named Ascendant).
