Does God of War Ragnarok have two endings?

Publish date: 2024-08-23

Yes, God of War Ragnarok has multiple endings and post-credits scenes. Here are some of the questions that you might have about the endings:

1. What are the secret endings in God of War Ragnarok?

While the story ‘ending’ releases you back into the world to chase endgame stuff and finish up loose ends, there’s another secret God of War Ragnarok ending that will roll the final full credits and play the Blood on the Snow God of War Ragnarok ending theme.

2. Can you still play God of War Ragnarok after the secret ending?

Yes, you can still play God of War Ragnarok after the secret ending. There’s no post-credits scene at the very end, but you do get to keep playing, so you don’t need to worry about being locked out of any quests.

3. Does God of War Ragnarok have an end?

Okay. The ending of God of War Ragnarok concludes the end of the Norse saga in that yes, Ragnarok does, in fact, come. Asgard is destroyed, and characters like Odin and Thor are dead, albeit not butchered by Kratos like the Greek pantheon were.

4. Can you get different endings in God of War?

Yes, after the game’s story comes to an end, you may think that’s all there is. However, God of War has a Marvel-esque hidden ending, one that you can only unlock after you complete the game’s final story mission and the credits roll.

5. What happens after beating God of War Ragnarok 100%?

After beating God of War Ragnarok 100%, there are several things you can still do to continue your gameplay experience. Some of them include participating in the Muspelheim Crucible, freeing the real Týr, battling secret bosses, and collecting various items such as Kvasir poems and Dvalin’s Lost pages.

6. Where is the real Tyr in God of War Ragnarok?

After the cataclysm ends in God of War Ragnarok, the real Tyr wakes up in Midgard. He walks along a spiral path, passing by all the friends he met in the game, until he gets to the top, where Angrboda is waiting.

7. Will Kratos go to Egypt in future God of War games?

In a spin-off comic titled “God of War: Fallen God,” it is revealed that during his exile, Kratos travels to Egypt. When Egyptian villagers see the Spartan, they immediately flee and hide, fearing for their lives.

8. Where did Atreus go after Ragnarok in God of War?

After the events of Ragnarok in God of War, Atreus wakes up in Midgard. He walks along a spiral path, passing by all the friends he met during his journey, until he reaches the top where Angrboda is waiting.

9. Was Odin Tyr the whole time in God of War Ragnarok?

Before the events of God of War Ragnarok, it is revealed that Tyr, the Norse god of war, who had been staying with Kratos and Atreus, has actually been Odin in disguise the whole time. Odin used his godly powers to disguise himself as Tyr, spying on Kratos, Atreus, and others.

10. Can Kratos lift Mjolnir in God of War Ragnarok?

No, Kratos cannot lift Mjolnir in God of War Ragnarok. The hammer cannot be wielded or picked up by Kratos or Atreus for various reasons.

11. Will Sindri forgive Atreus in God of War Ragnarok?

It seems unlikely that Sindri will forgive Atreus in God of War Ragnarok. After the incident involving the bear attack, Sindri distances himself from Atreus and refuses to forgive him.

12. Can you see Atreus after beating Ragnarok in God of War?

Yes, you can see Atreus again after completing the main story in God of War Ragnarok. Atreus will be with you on and off for the main game.

13. What prophecy did Kratos see at the end of God of War Ragnarok?

At the end of God of War Ragnarok, Kratos sees a Giant’s prophecy that he would die at Ragnarok. Kratos, however, scoffs at such predictions and believes that individuals make their own fate.

14. What happens to Tyr after Ragnarok in God of War Ragnarok?

In God of War Ragnarok, Tyr is revealed to have been imprisoned in Asgard the entire time. After the destruction of the realm throws him across the realms and into Niflheim, he can be found wandering in other realms after being set free.
