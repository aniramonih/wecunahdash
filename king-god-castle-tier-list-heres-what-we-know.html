King God Castle Tier List: Heres What We Know

Publish date: 2024-08-24

King God Castle is a kingdom defence gacha RPG in which you assign heroes to defend your castle against a series of invasions. In between battles you’ll use an in-game currency to summon more heroes, upgrade their abilities, and equip them with better gear. The game features a wide variety of heroes to collect, each with their own unique set of skills. There are also a number of different modes to play, including a survival mode and a boss rush mode.

If you’re looking for a kingdom defence game with plenty of content to keep you busy, then King God Castle is definitely worth checking out. In this game, you get to choose six heroes to defend your castle. You can earn gold and gems by playing through the PVE mode or battle other players in PVP.
