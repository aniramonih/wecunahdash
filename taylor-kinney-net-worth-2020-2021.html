Early Life

Taylor Kinney was born into a family of 6 living in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, to a dental hygienist mother Pamela and a banker father, Daniel Kinney. After the divorce of his parents, Taylor, along with his three brothers named Adam, Ryan, and Trent, grew up in Neffsville, Pennsylvania, with only his mother by his side.

After completing his school education from Lancaster Mennonite School, Taylor Kinney went on to procure a degree in Business Management from West Virginia University in Morgantown, West Virginia.

As for his personal life, Taylor Kinney met renowned singer Lady Gaga while filming the music video of her hit single ‘You and I’ after which they began to date and even went on to announce their engagement in 2015. Career

Taylor Kinney landed his first television show ‘Fashion House’ in 2006, where he went on to essay the role of Luke Gianni, one of the many characters involved in a web of lust, greed, and ambition enveloping the sensational takeover of a high profile fashion company.

Later that same year, he joined the cast of another television series called ‘Trauma.’ His slice of life performances got him noticed, and he got the opportunity to work in popular shows, like ‘The Vampire Diaries,’ ‘What About Brian,’ ‘CSI: NY,’ ‘Shameless’ and ‘Breakout Kings’ among others before landing his most iconic role of Lieutenant Kelly Severide in 2012.

Taylor Kinney has reprised the role of the Lieutenant in ‘Chicago P.D.’ and ‘Chicago Med,’ 2 other shows of the ‘Chicago Franchise’ so far and has not appeared in ‘Chicago Justice’ as yet. All these shows sort of cross over each other with many of the stories crossing paths at ‘Molly’s,’ a tiny bar that the characters from all the four series frequently visit.

Taylor Kinney made a seamless transition to the world of cinema in 2007 and has many memorable performances in films, like ‘Furnace,’ ‘Least Among Saints,’ ‘The Other Woman,’ ‘Rock the Kasbah’ and ‘The Forest’ to name a few to his credit. His most acclaimed performance, however, came in the 2012 political action thriller ‘Zero Dark Thirty, ’ which dealt with the decade-long search, discovery, and death of al Qaeda leader Osama bin Laden. As of 2017, Taylor Kinney is filming a movie called ‘Best Day of My Life,’ which is scheduled to release in 2018. He also has an innovative web series called ‘Dating Rules from My Future Self’ under his belt.
