Paul Wall shot to fame as a talented rapper throughout the 2000s and has been in a loving relationship with his wife, Crystal Wall, for nearly two decades. And during an interview, the rapper opened up about his tips for a long-lasting marriage.

Paul Wall's Wife Was Inspired to Become a Fitness Coach Following Her Own Health Journey

During an interview, Crystal revealed that her career in fitness stretches back to 2012, and since then, Paul's wife has found a way to make getting in shape fun — by dancing. Crystal said:

"I think as women, at some point in our lives, we stop dancing. [...] We forget to let loose and have just a good time, you know, and dance like nobody's watching."

Crystal's fitness journey began after she failed to lose her desired weight through dieting and even tried liposuction. However, she eventually found Zumba, which inspired her to become an instructor and helped Crystal lose 45 pounds.

Crystal and Paul have been married since 2005.

And at the time of writing, Paul's wife had amassed over 40,000 followers on Instagram, where she regularly shared videos of herself breaking a sweat in her fitness classes.

Paul Wall's Wife Brought Fitness to the Super Bowl

In 2017, Crystal brought her signature style of fun and fitness to the Super Bowl stage when she hosted the first-ever fitness concert in the form of multiple free live classes. The concept was the same as a typical concert, but instead of a performer, Crystal took the stage to lead a workout session. She said:

"We turn up. We want to dance like you dance at the club. This is a fitness party."

For Crystal, the number one goal was for attendees to have fun and channel their inner Beyoncé as musicians such as Pitbull and Migos played in the background. And among the faces in the crowd was Crystal's long-time friend Jill Scott.
