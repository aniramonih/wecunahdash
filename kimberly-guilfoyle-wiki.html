Kimberly Guilfoyle Wiki/Bio

Kimberly Ann Guilfoyle was born on March 9, 1969 in San Francisco, California, USA. She is an American television news presenter who also serves as an advisor to Donald Trump, the 45th President of the United States. She graduated from San Francisco High School and subsequently attended the University of California and the University of San Francisco. From 2000 until 2004, she worked as an assistant district attorney in San Francisco. She is a Republican Party member and has been Donald Trump Jr.'s girlfriend since 2018.

Kimberly Guilfoyle Wiki

Kimberly Ann Guilfoyle is a television news anchor from the United States. She worked for Fox News from 2006 until 2018, when she co-hosted The Five. She then became a member of America First Policies, a pro-Trump super PAC, and campaigned for Republicans in the 2018 midterm elections. She played a public defender in the 2004 film Happily Ever After, with Ed Asner and Marina Black, Shirley Temple's niece. The movie was shown at the Tribeca Film Festival.

She married Gavin Newsom, a San Francisco city supervisor at the time, in 2001. Guilfoyle married furniture heir Eric Villency on May 27, 2006, in Barbados. On October 4, 2006, she gave birth to their son, Ronan Anthony. Guilfoyle was diagnosed with COVID-19 in July 2020.
