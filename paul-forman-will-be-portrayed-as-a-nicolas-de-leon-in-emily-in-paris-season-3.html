In Season 3 of Netflix’s Emily in Paris, actor Paul Forman portrays Nicolas de Leon. Nicolas is an executive for his family’s company who is keen to show his value. Nicolas and Emily will create a particular relationship in the future season, according to Netflix’s Tudum, and it will be intriguing to watch how his role develops in the series. Paul Forman is a young and promising actor who has acted in The Spanish Princess and Frank of Ireland in small parts.
