Tragic Loss: Serenity Hawley and Ex-Boyfriend Died in Blacksburg Murder-Suicide

Publish date: 2024-08-13

In a devastating incident that shook the tight-knit community of Blacksburg, Virginia, a teenage girl’s life was tragically cut short by domestic violence. The victim, Serenity Hawley, a former student at Blacksburg High School, lost her life when she was shot by her ex-boyfriend, identified as Croney Monk. The incident unfolded on Tuesday, November 7, 2023.

Serenity Hawley and Ex-Boyfriend Died: The Tragic Incident

On that fateful day, the peaceful surroundings of Blacksburg were disrupted by a tragic incident that took place at 723 University City Blvd. It was approximately 7:45 p.m. when law enforcement agencies responded to a report of a violent altercation. Upon arriving at the scene, they discovered the lifeless bodies of two teenagers, Serenity Hawley and her ex-boyfriend, Croney Monk, inside a parking garage.

The investigators quickly determined that the cause of Serenity’s death was a gunshot wound inflicted by Croney Monk, who subsequently turned the weapon on himself, leading to his own demise. The incident has since been classified as a murder-suicide, leaving the community stunned and heartbroken.

Victims’ Identities

Serenity Hawley, just days away from celebrating her birthday, was the tragic victim of this incident. Croney Monk, known for his athleticism as a student at Blacksburg High School, was the perpetrator of this heinous act.

Police Response

The Blacksburg Police Department played a pivotal role in responding to the tragic incident. Their initial response was to a report of a suspicious vehicle at the aforementioned location, and upon reaching the scene, they discovered the lifeless bodies of Serenity and Croney inside the vehicle, along with the weapon used in the incident.

Both victims were identified as students enrolled at Blacksburg High School, and the law enforcement agencies, along with the Town of Blacksburg, have collaborated with Montgomery County Public Schools to provide necessary support to the staff and students who may require it. In accordance with their policy, the Blacksburg Police Department did not release the names of the juveniles involved in the incident.

Community Safety

The Blacksburg Police Department has assured the community that this incident is an isolated one, and there is no ongoing threat to the safety of the residents. However, they are continuing their active investigation to ensure that all aspects of this tragedy are thoroughly examined.
