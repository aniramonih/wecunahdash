How Old Is Janet Jackson's Son Eissa Al Mana Now? Know His Personal Life And Parent's Married Life

His mother is widely famous for her second soprano vocal to the top note "e" range, and her father is running the AI Mana Group of business, which provides brands related to home appliances and jewelry products.

Al Mana's Parents, Wissam & Janet Jackson's Marital Relationship

Eissa's biological parents, Wissam Al Mana and Janet Jackson were married in 2012. Their marriage was a private ceremony, where they welcomed their close relatives and friends only.

The couple met for the first time at a hotel located in Dubai, UAE in 2010. Shortly after their meeting, the duo began dating secretly. But, Wissam later proclaimed that they started dating from 2011. The pair then got engaged in just a few months earlier in 2012.

After marrying for four years, the pair gave a joint statement, where Jackson and her husband are now planning for their family upon her tour on 6th April 2016. She then confirmed that they're expecting their first child on 12th October 2016.

A year later, Jackson gave birth to their son, Eissa, in 2017. After the birth of their baby, Jackson and Wissam separated due to their cultural differences. In April 2017, they signed a divorce settlement worth $200 Million to Jackson, including child support for their son.

Eissa's Mother, Jackson's Past Marital Affairs & Relationship

Al Mana's mom, Janet, eloped with her first husband, James DeBarge, on 7th September 1984. More to that, Janet was three years younger than Debarge, who was 21-years-old at the time of their nuptial.

Two years upon the divorce, Jackson was involved in a romantic affair with an American rapper, Jermaine Dupri, in 2002. The duo stayed in a long-term relationship for seven years until they separated in 2009.
