Mark Wahlberg Dissing Jenny McCarthy & Donnie Wahlberg By Skipping Wedding?

Jenny and Donnie married on Aug. 30, and Mark expressed his congratulations later that day with the following tweet:

Congratulations @DonnieWahlberg and @JennyMcCarthy, so happy for you both today.

— Mark Wahlberg (@mark_wahlberg) August 30, 2014

To which Donnie replied:

Thank you @mark_wahlberg. Thanks to you & @rheadur for the adorable video for me & Jenny! Please wish a Happy B-Day to my god daughter Ella! — Donnie Wahlberg (@DonnieWahlberg) August 30, 2014

Jenny has yet to tweet back herself as of the morning of Aug. 31, though she hasn’t tweeted much of anything.

Mark Wahlberg & Wife Rhea Durham Allegedly Not Huge Fans Of Donnie Wahlberg’s Wife, Jenny McCarthy
