Who Are Top 10 Highest Paid WWE Wrestlers of All Time?

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Wrestling is considered to be one of the most entertaining sports programs on television today. For more than three decades the WWE ring has seen the start of many great wrestlers. Comparing the WWE contracts, the wording has changed very little for the benefits that wrestlers throughout the years have been provided. However, little is stated in the WWE contracts about the pay received by the wrestling stars. With a little research it has been found that the top 10 highest paid WWE wrestlers include:

List of the Highest Paid WWE Wrestlers

Vince McMahon’s Net Worth

Known as the king of the WWE Dynasty, Vince McMahon has spent his fair share of time in the ring. As a WWE wrestler Vince did win several titles for his performance. Combining his position as the WWE Chairman and his wrestling contract pay, Vince McMahon’s net worth is $750 million.

The Rock’s Net Worth

Dwayne Johnson is better known by his nickname the Rock. While the Rock is no longer a regular wrestler in the ring, during his time as a WWE wrestler and as an actor, the Rock’s net worth is $125 million.

Stone Cold Steve Austin’s Net Worth

Stone Cold Steve Austin is one of the best known WWE wrestlers of all time. While he is not seen in the ring these days, it is likely that Stone Cold Steve Austin’s name will be remembered for many years to come by true wrestling fans. Throughout his career Stone Cold Steve Austin’s net worth accumulated to be $45 million.
