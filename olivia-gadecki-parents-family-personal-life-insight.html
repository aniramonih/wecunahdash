Who Are Olivia Gadecki Parents?

Olivia is the daughter of emigrant parents. She was born on 24 April 2002 in Gold Coast, Australia.

The player’s mother Natalia Gadecki is Ukrainian and immigrated to Australia in 1993. However, her father has been absent from her life. Natalia’s sister and another family still live in now war-affected Ukraine.

Gadecki is the only daughter of her parents. She has five brothers named Daniel Gadecki, Davie Gadecki, Adam Gadecki, Andrew Gadecki, and Thomas Gadecki. One of her brothers, Thomas is also a tennis player with an ITF Junior Ranking of no. 341.


The 20-year-old has got a supportive mother and siblings. She wanted to be a tennis player after watching her brothers play.
