Who is Mischa Barton?

Mischa Barton was born on January 24, 1986, in Hammersmith, London. She grew up in New York City with her mother, Nuala, an Irish photographer, her father, Paul, an English foreign exchange broker, and her sisters, Hania and Zoe.

Mischa began performing in theatrical shows at the age of eight and won her first film role three years later. She graduated from Manhattan’s Professional Children’s School in 2004, and in the summer of 2006, she did an Acting Shakespeare course at London’s Royal Academy of Dramatic Art on the recommendation of her “Closing the Ring” director, Sir Richard Attenborough.

How many Philanthropy works has Mischa Barton supported?

Barton is very passionate about giving back to the community and reaching out to the less privileged.

Barton is the current spokesperson for Climate Star, a non-profit that tackles global warming through social and legislative engagement.
