Ryan Garcia announces new influencer girlfriend following divorce from model ex-wife Andrea Celina -

Publish date: 2024-08-24

American boxing star Ryan Garcia has announced he has a new girlfriend – less than two months after divorcing model ex-wife Andrea Celina.

Garcia, the former WBC interim lightweight champion, made an incredible double revelation early last month when he announced the birth of his second child with Celina and their divorce in a matter of minutes.

Court documents later showed that he did so due to ‘irreconcilable differences’ with his wife of three years, despite still promising to support their two children – three-year-old Bela and newly-born Henry – financially going forward.

And following his split from Celina, Garcia has wasted no time in moving on with his next partner.

In a post on social media, the 25-year-old gushed over new girlfriend Mikaela Testa – an Australian influencer with over 400,000 followers on Instagram.

The 25-1 fighter added: ‘Ima teach her how to fight! She’s the next one up @misfitsboxing’.

Last month, exclusive Dailymail.com pictures showed Celina leaving her ex-husband’s lavish $3million California home nearly three weeks after the divorce announcement.

Andrea – real name Andrea Celina Velarde – was seen at an upscale café close to the former couple’s luxury home with newborn son Henry strapped to her chest.

‘I’m deeply grateful for the years we shared and for her unwavering commitment to our family. ‘As we move forward, my focus remains on the health and wellbeing of our two beautiful kids. They are and will always be my first priority.
