Who was Sheila Oliver?

Sheila Y. Oliver was born on July 14, 1952. She held the position of second lieutenant governor of New Jersey.

She is a Democrat. In the 2013 special election, Oliver ran for the U.S. Senate.

Oliver was chosen as Phil Murphy’s running mate for lieutenant governor in the Democratic Party’s primary in November 2017 in July.
