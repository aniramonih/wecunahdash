Meet Thomas Payton, The Man Married To Sofia Mattsson

Thomas Payton is married to Swedish-born actress, Sofia Mattson, who only acquired US citizenship in February 2022. Apart from her long-running role of Sasha Gilmore on ABC drama General Hospital, she is best known for playing Natasha Williams in Campus Security from 2012 to 2013 and some other roles in Two And a Half Men,  NCIS: Naval Criminal Investigative Service, Becoming Bond, StartUp, and Drive Share. Sofia has also starred in a music video of Olly Murs’ song Heart Skips A Beat with Chiddy Bang.

Reportedly, Thomas and Sofia dated each other for 4 years before they got married on May 14, 2016. As for the wedding, the event took place on the beach in Santa Monica, California.

When Sofia’s character in General Hospital Sasha suddenly found herself pregnant after her night with Brando Corbin, the viewers doubted if the writers of the show we’re writing a real-life pregnancy into the script. Indeed, the viewers’ feeling was right as Sofia announced being pregnant through an Instagram post on June 14, 2021. She uploaded two pictures flawlessly flaunting her baby bump and wrote, “BIG things happening!!…but now that miss Sasha Gilmore’s pregnancy is in full force I can finally share that I’m so grateful to be sharing this pregnancy journey with her!”

Sofia and her husband welcomed their first child, a baby boy, on July 16, 2021. Sofia was again the first one to break the news of their child’s birth. She posted a picture with the newborn baby on her Instagram a month after the baby’s birth.

Thomas’ wife, Sofia, also went on month-long maternity in September 2021 to take care of their newly born baby and resumed work at General Hospital in October 2021.

The Paytons are smitten with each other and Sofia often features her husband Thomas in romantic posts.

In 2021’s Valentine, Sofia wrote a really sweet message to her husband Thomas and wished him happy Valentine’s day on Instagram. A little later that year, Sofia again wished Thomas a happy anniversary weekend.

What Does Thomas Payton Do For A Living?

According to Thomas Payton’s IMDB profile, he is an actor and writer by profession. As an actor, he has worked in projects such as I Lived, Campus Security, Only Sighing, among others.

Furthermore, Thomas wrote some of the episodes of Campus Security, which featured his wife Sofia. He has also penned short movies like Earth Therapy and Wynnewood Lane.

Thomas has one producer credit for Campus Security and one director credit for Wynnwood Lane.
