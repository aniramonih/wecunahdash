Early Life Of John McEnroe

John McEnroe, in complete John Patrick McEnroe Jr., was born on February 16, 1959, in Wiesbaden, West Germany.

According to the sources, John’s Father served in the United States Air Force & his mother worked as a surgical nurse there.

Reports suggest the world tennis legend grew up in Douglaston, New York, with his family, which included two younger brothers.

McEnroe picked up tennis at a young age since his Father was a recreational player.

He started playing organized tennis at nine, and within a few years, he was winning junior tournaments.

John Jr’s talent and dedication made him a top-ranked junior player in the United States and worldwide.

In addition to his tennis pursuits, McEnroe developed a passion for music and played guitar in a rock band.
