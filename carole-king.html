Age, Biography and Wiki

Carole King is an American singer-songwriter and pianist who has been active in the music industry since 1958. She is one of the most successful female singer-songwriters of all time, having sold more than 25 million albums and 100 million singles worldwide. She has won four Grammy Awards, including the Lifetime Achievement Award, and has been inducted into both the Songwriters Hall of Fame and the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame. King has released 25 studio albums, including Tapestry, which is one of the best-selling albums of all time. She has also written or co-written over 400 songs, many of which have been recorded by other artists, including Aretha Franklin, James Taylor, and The Beatles. In addition to her music career, King has also acted in several films and television shows.
