Brett Gardner And Wife Jessica Clendenin Are Big On Family Values

Publish date: 2024-08-09

Brett Gardner wife Jessica Clendenin is a philanthropist working in children welfare. Brett has two sons with her, Miller Gardner and Hunter Gardner.

Brett and Jessica met at College when he played for Charleston Cougars.

Jessica is also from an athletic family, as her elder brother, Joshua, is a former baseball player.

In addition, when they met, the 39-year-old was a three-year starter for the College of Charleston Cougars.

Later, the New York Yankees selected Gardner with the 109th overall selection in the third round of the 2005 Major League Baseball draft.

He had his Major League Baseball debut in 2008 and has been with the Yankees ever since. He has two All-Star appearances (2015, 2019) and a Gold Glove Award (2020). 

Inside Brett Gardner Married Life

Brett Gardner has been married to Jessica Clendenin since 2007. Brett had an intimate ceremony at First Presbyterian Church in Atlanta, Georgia. 

The couple celebrated their wedding day with friends and families all by their side who were present to share their joy and bestow the newlyweds on their new journey of life together.

Besides, their beautiful moments were captured by a professional photographer named Shannon Belletti. 

Some photographs uploaded on Jessica's brother's Facebook page highlight their carnival months before Jessica and Brett's nuptials.

It's been nearly 15 years since the pair married, and during that time, the couple has been frequently photographed attending various events together. Like when Brett and Jessica hosted a luncheon at Charleston Air Force Base in 2016.

During the 2016 Thanksgiving week, the couple showed their appreciation by organizing a special dinner at Charleston Air Force Base for more than 80 active duty personnel of the 15th Air Lift Squadron and their families, as per MLB. 

Jessica is frequently glimpsed strolling beside her husband on the red carpet, as she did at the College's 2014 Alumni Awards Gala. 

In 2020, Yankees player Brett sought a restraining order from a fan who dubbed herself his future fiancée. She described herself as Brett Gardner's "future missis" when she posted a photo of herself in a bridal gown. 

Clendenin Family Are From Georgia

Jessica Clendenin parents Tom and Susan Clendenin raised her in Kennesaw Georgia. Jessica has two brothers named Joshua and John Clendenin. 

John Clendenin's Facebook bio states that he attended Harrison High School, the University of Tennessee in Chattanooga, and Coastal Carolina University. 

According to Joshua "Josh" Clendenin's 2008 baseball roster description, he graduated from Harrison High School and attended Stetson University to study general business afterward. 

He also attended the College of Charleston, Northwest Florida State College, and Shorter College, where he served as a graduate assistant for the Shorter Haws baseball team. 

Per his LinkedIn profile, he was a former assistant baseball coach at the University of West Georgia. Josh is married to Jobria Clendenin, with whom he has one daughter. 

Brett Gardner Children

Gardner has two sons named Hunter and Miller Gardner with Jessica. Brett lives in South Carolina in the offseason with his family. 

Per Getty, Brett and Jessica attended the CCandy Children's Clothing Line Launch on August 8, 2013, at the MLB Fan Cave in New York City.

Well, now Gardner's children have gotten older, and being away from them has become quite difficult for him.

Additionally, Brett emphasized his bond with his family and children in an interview with the MLB. He expressed gratitude that his children are old enough to recall most of their family's adventures.

He went on to explain that he hopes the individuals they've met and the things they've learned from his colleagues or their families will benefit them and give them a more well-rounded outlook on life. 

More On The Gardner Family

Brett Gardner parents are Jerry and Faye Gardner. Brett grew up in a farming household residing in Holly Hock South Carolina. 

His father, Jerry, is a farmer who owns a 2,600-acre farm and has spent his life nurturing it. Brett grew up helping his dad with farm chores and kept an eye on the weather. 

He told the Daily News how fascinated he is with the weather and how it has always affected his life. 

Moreover, he also has a brother, Glen Gardner, who resides in downtown Charleston.
