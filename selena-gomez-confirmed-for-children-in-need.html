Selena Gomez Confirmed for Children in Need

Publish date: 2024-08-11

Pop beauty Selena Gomez has been confirmed as a performer for the annual fundraising show Children In Need.

Selena will be performing her new single ‘Same Old Love’ on Friday.

The 23 year old will take to the stage on the 35th annual telethon fundraising juggernaut, over the years Children In Need have raised over an incredible £600 million for disadvantaged children.

Years and Years will also be performing their hit single ‘Eyes Shut’ and Ellie Goulding will be performing her upcoming track ‘Army’.

There are also a host of other big names set to perform, along with TV shows getting involved with special Children In Need sketches.

The show will again be hosted by Sir Terry Wogan.

Children In Need will be on BBC 1 from 7.30pm on Friday 13th November.
