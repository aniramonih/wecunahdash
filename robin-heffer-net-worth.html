What is Robin Heffer's Net Worth?

Robin Heffer is a British businessman who has a net worth of $48 million. Robin Heffer is the cofounder of London-based RWM Food Group Ltd., a food-processing company, in the United Kingdom and internationally, which engages in beef and lamb production, beef and lamb processing, beef and lamb sales, chilled distribution, darfresh and tri-web packaging, as well as food service and retail. The company serves retail, wholesale, export and food service customers. Its sous vide products include beef casseroles steaks and ales, beer, bacon and roasted red onions, lamb hot pots, red wine and rosemary shanks, bbq pork ribs and bolognaise.

The company was founded in 1968 and is based in Langport, United Kingdom. As of October 7, 2011, RWM Food Group Ltd. began operating as a subsidiary of Anglo Beef Processors (UK) Limited, a company specializing in renewable, pet food and protein divisions, which combine to ensure that the value of by-products is maximized and the environmental impact of their business and customers is minimized.
