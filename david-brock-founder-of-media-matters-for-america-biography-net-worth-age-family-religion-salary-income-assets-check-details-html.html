A Transformation in Ideology

David Brock, the brain behind Media Matters for America, has undergone a significant shift in his beliefs. Media Matters: Unraveling Conservative Misinformation

Media Matters for America operates as a non-profit watchdog, tirelessly working to identify and rectify conservative misinformation in the media. Its mission is clear: correct biased reporting, challenge falsehoods, and stand against unjust criticism directed at progressive causes and figures. By keeping a close eye on conservative news outlets, radio programs, and television shows, Media Matters fulfills its mission effectively.
