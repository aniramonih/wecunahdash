Who are Kevin Gates' kids?

Publish date: 2024-08-29

RAPPER Kevin Gates achieved fame thanks to his hits such as 2 Phones and Really Really.

Aside from rap music, fans are curious to know more about Kevin and his life as a father.

Who are Kevin Gates' kids?

In October 2015, Kevin Gates married his then-girlfriend Dreka Haynes.

The pair are parents to two children together, a daughter named Islah and a son named Kazah.

Kevin's true fans might have noticed that he named his debut studio album after his daughter and his third record as a tribute to his son.

The hip-hop hitmaker also claims to be a father figure to an unspecified number of other children as well.

Regarding his take on parenthood, Kevin told HotNewHipHop: "I got partners that got life sentences at a young age and I had to step up to the plate and take care of their children. I’ve always been a caretaker of young people.

"If I wasn’t doing this [referring to his career], I wouldn’t be able to provide for my children. So I would be laying down on them…it puts things into perspective."

The Louisiana-based star shared similar sentiments with Complex and said: "I got some children. I'm real, real close with them.

"I lay in the bed with them, hold them, love on them. It really doesn't make sense to say [how many kids I have]."
