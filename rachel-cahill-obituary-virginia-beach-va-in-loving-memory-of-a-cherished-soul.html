Rachel Cahill Obituary, Death – Rachel Elizabeth Cahill, aged 85, of Burton, MI, passed away on June 25, 2005, surrounded by her loving family at Genesys Health Park. Her life was one of unwavering devotion, kindness, and love, leaving an indelible mark on all who had the privilege of knowing her. Born on May 15, 1920, in Tompkins Center, MI, Rachel was the beloved daughter of George and Audrey Southworth. From an early age, she exemplified compassion and selflessness, traits that would define her throughout her life. Rachel was a devout member of Holy Rosary Catholic Church, where her faith and spirituality guided her every action.

A Cherished Family Woman

Rachel was a devoted wife, mother, and grandmother, finding immense joy and fulfillment in caring for her family. Her unwavering love and support were the foundation upon which her family thrived. Survived by her son, Maurice, and daughter, Janice, along with their spouses Janet Cahill and David Stump, Rachel’s legacy of love continues through her grandchildren, Jesse, Shelbe, Katherine, and Eric. Rachel’s life was not without its share of challenges, including the loss of her beloved husband, William, in 1998, and her son, Steven, in 1982. Despite these hardships, Rachel remained resilient, drawing strength from her faith and the love of her family. Her brothers, Max and Maurice, along with a host of extended family and friends, mourn her passing while cherishing the memories she left behind.
