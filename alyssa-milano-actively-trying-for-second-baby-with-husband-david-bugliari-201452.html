Alyssa Milano &quot;Actively Trying&quot; to Get Pregnant With Second Baby

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Alyssa Milano's ready for baby No. 2! The Mistresses actress, who welcomed son Milo with husband David Bugliari back in 2011, is hoping to give her 2-year-old tot a little brother or sister sooner rather than later, she told Access Hollywood Live on Monday, Feb. 3.

"We are actively trying," the 41-year-old Who's the Boss? alum revealed. Then, joking, she added: "[David's] just chugging down ginseng." (Some research suggests that ginseng can increase sex drive and improve erectile function in men.)

Milano noted, however, that her romantic encounters with her husband are very organic. "They're very natural moments," she clarified. "It's not like we're machines or biological experiments. We're very attracted to each other."
