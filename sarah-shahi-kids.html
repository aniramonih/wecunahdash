Sarah Shahi Has Three Kids With This Well-Known Actor

Publish date: 2024-08-22

Who does 'Sex/Life' and 'Red, White and Royal Blue' star Sarah Shahi have kids with? Is it Adam Demos or her ex-husband? Here's what to know.

But what about her life off-screen? Here's what we know about Sarah Shahi offscreen, including her adorable children and their dad.

Who does 'Sex/Life' star Sarah Shahi have kids with?

Sarah actually has three children, two sons and one daughter. Her firstborn son, William Wolf Howey, was born in 2013. Her other son and daughter, twins Knox Blue Howey and Violet Moon Howey, were born in 2015.

The actress shares her three kiddos with ex-husband Steve Howey. (If you're a Shameless fan you'll recognize Steve for his role as Kevin Ball!) If not, you might remember him from his hilarious role as Van on the hit show Reba, or from his memorable appearance in the romantic comedy Bride Wars.

Article continues below advertisement

Why did Sarah divorce her ex-husband, Steve Howey?

Steve and Sarah were one of those under-the-radar celebrity couples that had been married for years, hence why fans of the two actors were shocked when they announced their divorce in June 2020 after 11 years of marriage.

True to any notable Hollywood split, the couple provided an official statement about their conscious uncoupling to Us Weekly.

Article continues below advertisement

The pair wrote, "After much thought and consideration, we have decided to end our marriage. We are so grateful for the past 10 years together and proud of our children that we’ve raised. As we enter into this new phase of our lives, our priority will remain coparenting our children with so much love, mutual respect, and friendship. And though our relationship is changing, we are committed to continuing our lives as a loving family.”

Sarah Shahi fell for Steve Howey on the set of another TV series.

Back when Steve was on the hit show Reba, Sarah appeared on an episode in 2004 as a guest-star. Sparks flew and Sarah and Steve eventually went on to tie the knot in Las Vegas in 2009. They had been engaged for two years prior to their actual nuptials.

Article continues below advertisement

Although Steve and Sarah have kept details about their divorce relatively private, Steve mentioned in a 2016 Talk Nerdy With Us interview (via The Things) that, "When we had the kids, it was really tough. We’re both working actors, so we were very tired, and when we’d come back, if there was any energy left, it was for the kids, and not for each other."

The couple share custody of their three children.

Sarah went on to date her Sex/Life co-star Adam Demos, whom she met in 2020. It's unclear when they first began dating, but they went public with their romance in December 2020.
