Some Lesser Known Facts About Geetha Madhuri

  • Does Geetha Madhuri smoke?: No
  • Does Geetha Madhuri drink alcohol?: No
  • Geetha Madhuri is the only child of her parents.
  • She started learning classical music at a very young age under Smt. Kocherlakota Padmavati Garu.
  • She later joined the ‘Little Musicians Academy’ where she got trained in light music under Ramachari Garu.
  • Geetha participated in a singing reality show ‘SYE Singers Challenge’ that was aired on ETV Telugu and was one of the semi-finalists.
  • She also participated in a singing reality show ‘Super Singer Season 7’ that was aired on MAA TV.

  • Till date, she has sung more than 550 songs. Some of them are ‘Pakka Localo’ of film ‘Janatha Garage’ (2016), ‘Darlingey’ of film ‘Mirchi’ (2013), ‘Top Lesipoddi’ of film ‘Iddarammayilatho’ (2013), ‘O My God’ of film ‘Bodyguard’ (2012), etc.
  • Geetha shared screen space with her husband in a Telugu short film ‘Aditi.’

  • She has sung in different languages like Telugu, Tamil, Malayalam, and Kannada.
  • She has also sung numerous devotional songs like ‘Govinda Namalu’ of album ‘Govinda Namalu’, ‘Madhuram Sri Shirdi Sai’ and ‘Naadho Needho’ of album ‘Sri Shirdi Sai’, ‘Muddula Ayyappa’ of album ‘Ayyappa’, etc.
  • Geetha was popularly known as the ‘Shreya Ghoshal of South.’
  • She gave the live performances at various stage shows in almost all over the world.
  • She is fond of sarees.
  • In 2018, she participated in the controversial reality TV show ‘Bigg Boss Telugu Season 2.’
