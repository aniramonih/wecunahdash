Alejandro Garnacho Career and Net Worth

Alejandro first joined the Spanish club Atletico Madrid’s youth system in 2015 from Getafe. Later in October 2020, he joined the Manchester United Academy. Eventually, the wonderkid penned his first professional contract with the team in July 2021.

After playing with the youth team, Alejandro made his first-team debut for United as a substitute on 28 April 2022 in a 1-1 draw against Chelsea. He then made his first senior start for United in a 3-0 win over Sheriff Tiraspol in the UEFA Europa League on 27 October 2022.

Alejandro registered his first senior goal in a Europa League match against Real Sociedad. Besides, he registered his first Premier League goal on 13 November 2022, helping United grasp a last-minute winner against Fulham.

As he is eligible to play for both Spain and Argentina, Alejandro first featured for the Spain under-18 team in 2021. He also played for the Argentina under-20 side.
