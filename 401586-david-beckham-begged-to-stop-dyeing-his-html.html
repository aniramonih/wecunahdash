What Were David's Hair Transformations

Throughout his life, David has had many hair transformations. Each time his fans have shared their feelings about his haircuts, whether they think they are lovely or atrocious.

David Beckham opens the David Beckham Football Academy at the Greenwich peninsula, London, UK | Source: Getty Images

In 2000, he had a close-cropped buzz cut when he was approaching the height of his fame. However, by 2002, he had grown his hair out to cover his neck and ears and had blonde highlights.

The look was a favorite, especially among his female fans. Yet when 2005 came, his looks had changed once again. His hair was longer in the back but had been trimmed in the front, giving him a mullet-like look.

He had also yet to maintain the color he had added in earlier years, which somewhat led to his hair yellowing. His natural hair color peeked through the highlighted parts, resulting in a messy hairstyle.

David and Brooklyn Beckham attend "A Night Of Heroes: The Sun Military Awards" at The Imperial War Museum | Source: Getty Images

However, by 2008 he had reverted to his buzz cut and natural hair color. After growing it out for a while, he had a mohawk-style cut with just the sides of his head buzzed, and the rest left long. Once again, he went for highlighted ends.

In 2010, David had somewhat returned to his mullet roots, but this time his hair was longer all over. He had also not colored his roots in a long time, leaving his natural hair color to peek through.

Four years later, the ex-footballer gave into the craze of combovers and cut his hair short on the sides and longer on the top, sweeping his hair from one side of his head to the other to create the desired look.

David Beckham arrives at the amfAR Gala Cannes 2017 at Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc on May 25, 2017 in Cap d'Antibes, France | Source: Getty Images

David waited another four years before his following drastic hairstyle change and, in 2018, gave in to the next craze, which was the all-important man bun. Once again, David flaunted his natural hair color for this look.

Yet he soon cut his hair into a more toned-down one of close-cropped sides and a short top. Now, he has grown his hair again, with it long all over and dyed an almost bleached blonde.

Harper Beckham and David Beckham attend the Victoria Beckham FW23 show during Paris fashion week on March 03, 2023 in Paris, France | Source: Getty Images

While his fans have loved and supported him through each phase of his life and the hair transformation that came with it, David's hair is not the only aspect of himself that has evolved.

David Went Through Style Transformations Alongside Victoria

The Spice Girls alum and ex-footballer tied the knot in 1999 and quickly climbed the ranks as one of the world's most beloved celebrity couples. Their style transformation was closely watched by adoring fans.

David Beckham and his wife British pop star Victoria Beckham attend the Versace store party held at New Bond Street on June 11, 1999 in London | Source: Getty Images

In 2003, the couple had a sporty style, often rocking sweats and trucker hats. Yet when they wanted to ramp up the formality, they stepped out in matching black blazers, white shirts, and jeans.

When they went utterly formal, they did so beautifully. The couple received the Order of the British Empire from Queen Elizabeth II in 2003 and stepped out in matching black outfits, complete with a double-breasted suit for David and a feathered fascinator for Victoria.

By 2004 the couple each had a new hairstyle and a style evolution to go along with it. Still fans of coordinating their outfits, they showed up to a red carpet-event with Victoria in a black dress and David in a black blazer.

David and Victoria Beckham arrive at the Laureus World Sports Awards on May 16, 2005 at the Estoril Casino, Estoril, Portugal | Source: Getty Images

David and Victoria Beckham attend the "My Sky HD Wears Fendi" cocktail party as part of Milan Fashion Week Autumn/Winter 2009/2010 Menswear on January 20, 2009 in Milan, Italy | Source: Getty Images

In 2005 they had a new hairstyle and changed their style to match the rapidly evolving fashion trends of the early 2000s. Victoria was seen in a maxi skirt and corseted top, while David wore an open-breasted suit. This was also the year the couple had matching highlights in their hair.

2006 was the year the couple ramped up their style from casual and fun to formal, classy, and elegant. Victoria had extensions installed in her hair and began carrying Birkin bags, while David went from wearing sweatpants often to wearing blazers almost always.

The couple had become so famous by 2009 that they shot an underwear campaign for Emporio Armani together. Fans loved the couple's campaign and their style evolution for the photoshoot.

Victoria Beckham and David Beckham are seen on March 04, 2023 in Paris, France | Source: Getty Images

In 2018, the couple briefly returned to their street-style routes and posed in matching jackets and trousers for Paris Fashion Week. They have now evolved their styles to fit today's fashion standards.
