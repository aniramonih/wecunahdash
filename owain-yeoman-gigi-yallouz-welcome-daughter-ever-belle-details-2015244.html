Owain Yeoman, Gigi Yallouz Welcome Daughter Ever Belle: Details

Publish date: 2024-08-11

Such happy news! The Mentalist alum Owain Yeoman and his wife Gigi Yallouz welcomed their first child together on Friday, April 24, Us Weekly can exclusively reveal. 

The Welsh star, 36, is now the father to a baby daughter named Ever Belle. A rep for Yeoman tells Us, "Ever Belle Yeoman was born today weighing 6 pounds, 11 ounces and 19 inches tall. Gigi and Owain are over the moon."

The TURN: Washington's Spies actor married jewelry designer Yallouz in September 2013. Yeoman and Yallouz announced their pregnancy last fall, as the actor shared his excitement with an ultrasound shot last October.
