The Views Ana Navarro reveals candid details about weight loss journey, including failed diet of

Publish date: 2024-08-11

THE VIEW host Ana Navarro has opened up about her dieting attempts and how she's kept off the pounds in recent months.

Ana took to Instagram to share how she stays healthy on airplane food spending 14-16 hours a week commuting between her Miami home and The View studios in New York.

The View host posted a photo of a colorful meal spread on her Instagram.

Her airplane meal included grains, greens, and root veggies.

Ana gushed: "One of the many little conscious life-changes I’ve made to lose weight, is to buy a salad at airport or pre-order the healthy meal on American Air.

"Pre-ordering means I’m stuck with my choice and don’t fall into temptation."

Ana continued to divulge: "Truthfully, I don’t love it. I’ve also stopped drinking alcohol on planes. It adds up for me, since I usually spend 14-16 hours a week on a plane."

The host continued to discuss her struggles in the comments, replying and interacting with many of her fans.

One fan commented: "Looks delicious. I recently lost 23 pounds on Weight Watchers. You're looking great!"

Ana replied, sharing her struggles with the popular weight loss community.

She said: "I tried Weight Watchers. We had a group going at The View for a while.

"But I was never great at tracking points. I'd end up spending all of my points on booze and then having oranges for dinner."

Ana added a laughter emoji as other fans reacted to her story.

UNDER THE NEEDLE

"When I told [The View stylist Fran Taylor] I was going with @sarahaines to a drag brunch in Miami Beach this weekend, she said: 'I have just the thing.'

"And here you have it. 'Drag-Brunch Chic.'"
