WWE Saturday Nights Main Event Results From Montgomery, AL (4/23/22)

Publish date: 2024-08-20

The WWE RAW roster is on the road this weekend in Alabama and tonight they were in Montgomery for Saturday Night’s Main Event. Check out the results of the show below courtesy of Twitter user @young_ent_.

  • Riddle defeated The Miz
  • Veer defeated R-Truth in a squash match
  • United States Championship: Theory defeated Finn Balor
  • KO Show with Special Guest Ezekiel
  • Cody Rhodes defeated Seth Rollins
  • The Street Profits defeated Alpha Academy
  • Bobby Lashley defeated Omos by DQ after Omos used a chair
  • WWE RAW Women’s Championship: Bianca Belair defeated Becky Lynch and Liv Morgan
