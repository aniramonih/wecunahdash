Update On Maria/Playboy, Royal Rumble/HD Notes, Torrie

Publish date: 2024-08-06


The latest issue of Power Slam magazine out of the U.K. (January 2008 issue) notes that WWE Diva Maria Kanellis recently stated that she would like to be the next WWE Diva to pose for Playboy.

The HD broadcast of the Royal Rumble will be airing on digital cable, as well as DirecTV. Royal Rumble being broadcast in HD has been confirmed as the iNDEMAND website is listing that the Rumble will air on their HD PPV channel. If you have an HD PPV channel, you should be able to see the Rumble on HD. However, the HD feed is priced at $49.95 instead of $39.95 for the Standard Definition broadcast.

Torrie Wilson has posted an update on her back issues in her latest blog. Wilson wrote, “On the health front, my back problems don’t seem to be getting any better and I am actually starting to feel worse believe it or not! It’s gotten to the point that I can noly stand up in the morning. I’ve never really been hurt before so this is really REALLY annoying to me. I can’t stand feeling frail and just want to go back to my active lifestyle. I am looking into another type of treatment right now that looks promising so let’s hope this problem goes away soon. Please pray for me! I have actually been out of the action in the wrestling ring for quite some time and am really starting to miss it.”
