Ronnie Wayne Floyd (born November 11, 1955) has been the Senior Pastor of Cross Church in Springdale, Arkansas, since 1986. Dr. Floyd was elected as the 60th president of the Southern Baptist Convention on June 11, 2014, at the conclusion of the convention’s annual meeting. Cross Church has grown to a five-campus ministry that touches thousands of people each week under Floyd’s leadership. Floyd’s blog, podcast, and television ministry also reach people all over the world. Floyd is the founder of the Cross Church School of Ministry, a one-year residential ministry experience that trains leaders for life, ministry, and global Gospel promotion. He is a prolific author and Southern Baptist leader. Floyd is the creator and host of the Summit, a weekly lunch conference for Northwest Arkansas businesspeople that features national speakers. Floyd cultivates leadership traits in the next generation of pastors and ministers on his website, ronniefloyd.com.
