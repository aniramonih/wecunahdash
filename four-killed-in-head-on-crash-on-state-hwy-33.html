Four killed in head on crash on State HWY 33

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Driggs, ID (CBS2) — In Teton County, four people from Utah were killed in a head-on crash on Sept. 1.

Idaho State Police (ISP) are investigating the crash that happened just before 10 pm on Friday.

ISP says the crash occurred when a 2022 Tesla driven by a 46-year-old Mantua, Utah woman heading eastbound on State Highway 33 just west of Driggs crossed the center line and collided head-on with a 2007 Kenworth commercial vehicle that was driven by a 44-year-old Rexburg man that was traveling westbound HWY 33.

The driver of the Tesla and her three passengers, a 22-year-old Kaysville, Utah woman, a 24-year-old Kaysville, Utah man, and a child, died at the scene.

Everyone involved in the crash was wearing their seat belts.
