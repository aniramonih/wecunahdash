What is Rebecca Sugar's Net Worth?

Rebecca Sugar is an American animation director, screenwriter, cartoonist, and songwriter who has a net worth of $4 million. Rebecca Sugar was born in Silver Spring, Maryland in July 1987. She created the television series Steven Universe for the Cartoon Network and became the first woman to create a series independently for that network. From 2009 to 2012 Sugar worked on the Cartoon Network TV series Adventure Time as a writer, storyboard artist, storyboard revisionist, songwriter, and voice actor.

Since 2012 she has been the executive producer, storyboard artist, storyline writer, and songwriter for the series Steven Universe. Sugar has been nominated for two Primetime Emmy Awards for Steven Universe and two more for Adventure Time with Finn & Jake. She has been romantically linked to Ian Jones-Quartey. Sugar has also worked on the shorts The Classroom Gems: Fusion, Steven Universe: Wacky Sack Hot Dog Duffel Bag, The Classroom Gems: How Are Gems Made?, Steven Universe: We Are the Crystal Gems, and The Classroom Gems: What Are Gems?.
