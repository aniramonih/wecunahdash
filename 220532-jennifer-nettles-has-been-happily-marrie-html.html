Jennifer Nettles Has Been Happily Married to Justin Miller since 2011 Glimpse into Their Love St

Publish date: 2024-08-06

Country musician Jennifer Nettles is happily married to Justin Miller. The pair has been together since 2011 and her career was the one that led to the pair meeting.

In 2006, Nettles was still married to her first husband, Todd Van Sickle. As a part of the band Sugarland, she and her musical partner, Kristian Bush, were shooting a music video for one of their songs.

They were filming for "Want To" which happened to feature a former model named Justin Miller. The former model was hired to play Nettles’ lover in the music video.

Justin Miller and Jennifer Nettles at the 53rd Academy of Country Music Awards in Las Vegas on April 15, 2018 | Photo: Michele Crowe/CBS/Getty Images

DESTINED TO BE TOGETHER

At one point, the pair even ended up sharing a bed together. The video was somewhat a premonition of what was to come for the singer and the former model.

In 2007, Nettles and Van Sickle separated leaving her single again after seven years of marriage. The country star wasn’t in a rush to get back to dating and she only started seeing Miller in 2009.

NETTLES’ MARRIAGE TO MILLER

Two years later and the couple then got married two years later on November 26, 2011. At Blackberry Farm, the country musician and Miller tied the knot in a small ceremony in the Smoky Mountains of Tennessee.

A SMALL AND PRIVATE WEDDING

Their private nuptials were attended by a few friends and family and bandmate Bush. Miller was a self-established man who ran his own business.

THE COUPLE’S ONLY CHILD

The couple is quite private when it comes to their personal lives. They are parents to one child, a son Magnus Hamilton who was born in December 2012.

SPOTTED TOGETHER

One of the rare times that Nettles and Miller were seen together was on November 9, 2011, before their marriage. The entrepreneur accompanied his musician wife to the CMA Awards where Sugarland won an award for Vocal Duo of the Year.

WHAT MAKES JENNIFER HAPPY

Sugarland has also won several Grammy Awards. In a previous interview, Nettles revealed how her son’s laughter and "the smell of my husband’s neck in the morning" were among things that made her happy.
