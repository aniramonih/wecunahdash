Jason Ruszin Died In A Severe Road Accident In West Grove, West Virginia

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Jason Ruszin, a recent high school graduate, tragically lost his life in a severe road accident on December 27th in West Grove, West Virginia.

Who Was Jason Ruszin?

Jason Ruszin, affectionately known as JJ, was a beloved resident of West Grove, celebrated for his infectious smile and friendly demeanour. Fresh out of high school, JJ was deeply rooted in the community, actively engaging in local activities and events. Known for his altruism, he consistently extended a helping hand, leaving a positive mark on everyone he encountered. His radiant personality and kind-hearted nature endeared him to those fortunate enough to know him.

The Incident:

The tragic accident happened when JJ was travelling east on East Baltimore Road. Sadly, the weather took a severe turn for the worse, with rain intensifying as he navigated the road. In the midst of the deluge, he tragically lost control of his vehicle, veering off the roadway and colliding with a concrete wall and a utility pole. The vehicle caught fire, and despite the prompt response from emergency services, Jason Ruszin was pronounced dead at the crash site at 10:46 p.m. on December 27th.

The West Grove Fire Company, Medic 94, EMS, and the Chester County Coroner’s Officer immediately responded to the accident scene. Crews worked tirelessly for several hours to fix a damaged utility pole on the West Baltimore Pike. The investigation into the crash is still ongoing, with PSP Avondale in charge. Authorities are requesting that anybody with information contact Trooper Carter at 610-268-2022.
