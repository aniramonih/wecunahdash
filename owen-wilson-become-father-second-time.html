Owen Wilson 'expecting a baby' with fitness instructor Caroline Lindqvist

Publish date: 2024-08-30

    star Owen Wilson is to become a father for the second time.

    Two years after the birth of his first child, the actor, 44, is preparing to welcome a baby with personal trainer Caroline Lindqvist, a source has confirmed to .

    Caroline is currently embroiled in a divorce with her plastic surgeon husband. She and Owen, who met several years ago when he hired her to be his trainer, are not currently in a relationship — but he has made it clear he is there for her and their baby.

    "Owen is very involved with the pregnancy," a source explained. "He isn't in a relationship with Caroline, nor do they plan to be in one, but he is very supportive of everything.

    "They see each other and talk often about what they want for their child. Owen is very open-minded about everything and has told Caroline he will do whatever he can."

    The new arrival will be Owen's second baby. He is already dad to Robert Ford, 2, with ex-girlfriend Jade Duell.

    Speaking shortly after the birth of his son on 14 January, 2011, the star joked, "He's not, I wouldn't say, a wonderful conversationalist at this point, but he kind of doesn't have to be, he's so cute.

    "He becomes a sort of little scene stealer when he come in the room.

    "(He's like) Steve McQueen in The Magnificent Seven, where he didn't have a lot of dialogue — you just kind of gave him the camera and he was able to hold the screen. I feel like Ford is heading in that direction."
