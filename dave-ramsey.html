Who exactly is Dave Ramsey?

Dave Ramsey is a personal finance adviser and radio show host from the United States. He is also a businessman and an author.

As a radio host, he was the host of The Ramsey Show.

Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, and Education of Dave Ramsey

His birthday is September 3, 1960, and he was born David Lawrence Ramsey III in Antioch, Tennessee, USA. His mother’s and father’s names are not revealed.

Dave is of Caucasian origin, and he attended Antioch High School before enrolling at the University of Tennessee.

He graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Finance and Real Estate from the latter.

Dave Ramsey’s Professional Life and Career

Dave Ramsey began his career as a financial recovery agent before transitioning into financial advice. To accomplish this, he established the Lampo Group (now Ramsey Solutions) in 1988.

He was also a radio host for The Money Game, Fox Business, and The Dave Ramsey Show. He advised folks who were in debt to use the debt snowball method.

Dave has now published five adult books as well as six children’s books. His first book, named Financial Peace, was released in 1992. Furthermore, The Total Money Makeover is a New York Times best-seller.
