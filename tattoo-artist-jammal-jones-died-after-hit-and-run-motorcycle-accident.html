Tattoo Artist Jammal Jones Died After Hit-and-Run Motorcycle Accident

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Jammal Jones, a famous tattoo artist from Atlanta, died a few days after getting severely injured in a hit-and-run motorcycle accident. Read on to learn how did Jammal Jones die, what happened to him, and what was the cause of his untimely death.

The highly skilled tattoo artist was struggling for his life at a hospital for the last few days. He was in the ICU and a fundraiser was also launched to support his treatment. Unfortunately, he couldn’t recover and passed away this morning.

Who was Jammal Jones?

Jammal Jones was a tattoo artist, painter, and barber from Atlanta, Georgia. He was also a well-known motorcycle rider in the community. Jammal was the son of Lerone Bouldin. His work was widely appreciated online as he had over 41.5k Instagram followers.

He frequently shared his impressive work on the platform and people loved it. Jammal was such a great tattoo artist that he got clients from other states and even countries. He didn’t meet anyone without an appointment.

Jammal Jones Motorcycle Accident: What happened?

Tattoo artist Jammal Jones was involved in a scary motorcycle accident on July 31, 2023, at an intersection. He was right across the street from his apartments at the time of the crash. He wasn’t driving recklessly.

Unfortunately, he got hit by a speeding automobile which then fled the area. Due to the impact, Jammal was thrown off his bike and landed badly on the concrete road. He got a lacerated liver, a broken leg in the thigh and calf area, amputation on the left hand, and fractures in the spine.

He also suffered a broken hip, brain bleed, and swelling. Jammal was rushed to the hospital and admitted to the ICU after the motorcycle accident. The doctors put him in a medically induced coma to keep his condition stable.

Jamal Jones Died Due to Fatal Injuries

After fighting for his life in the ICU for nearly a week, Jammal Jones died due to the injuries sustained in the hit-and-run motorcycle accident. He suffered several injuries to his leg, hand, hip, spine, and head. These became the cause of Jammal Jones’ death.

Lerone Bouldin, the father of Talmond Jammal Jones, announced the tragic news of his son’s death with an emotional Facebook post. He revealed in it that the trauma to Jammal’s head was so severe that his brain had swollen to the point it began to herniate.

Eventually, he passed away and must be at peace right now. An investigation is going on to catch the culprit behind Jammal Jones’ death. We will update this post when more details are released by the local officers.

Obituary for Jammal Jones Awaited & Funeral Pending

Jammal Jones was an extremely talented tattoo artist and a passionate motorcycle rider. He was also a rising social media star. His work was appreciated in every corner of the world. Whoever saw his tattoos, instantly liked them.
