Doug Batchelor Amazing Facts

Established in 1965 by Joe Crews in Baltimore, Maryland, Amazing Facts had its roots in Joe Crews’ admiration for the success of Paul Harvey’s “The Rest Of The Story.” Initially conceived to engage both Christian and non-Christian audiences, the goal of Amazing Facts was to deliver daily 15-minute programs. These programs commenced with intriguing scientific or historical facts, connecting them to broader Biblical messages. READ: Doug Hitchner

Over time, the initiative expanded to include home Bible study courses and books authored by Joe Crews. In 1987, Amazing Facts leaped television ministry, leading to the development of four programs and periodic evangelism series.

Joe Crews, before his passing in 1994, passed the mantle to Doug Batchelor, who assumed the role of president and continues to hold the position today. The flagship radio program, “Bible Answers Live,” now primarily airs from Granite Bay, California, reaching approximately 100 national stations every Sunday evening.

In a significant development at the close of 2018, Amazing Facts transitioned into an independent ministry and rebranded itself as Amazing Facts International.
