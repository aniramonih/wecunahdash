Rosanna Pansino is an American YouTuber, actress, author, singer, baker, producer, and voice artist who is best known for her internet cooking show Nerdy Nummies. It became the most popular cooking series on YouTube and won her a Shorty Award in 2013 as well as an additional nomination in 2015, and earned her 5 Streamy Award nominations too. One of the highest-paid women on YouTube, she also peaked Forbes‘ “Top Influencers: Food” list in 2017. Moreover, Rosanna Pansino has released songs like Perfect Together and Rainbow Magic (ft. Schmoyoho) as well as authored books titled The Nerdy Nummies Cookbook and Baking All Year Round.

    Rosanna Pansino Facts

  • She started making YouTube videos as a sort of practice to polish up her acting skills. She felt that making such videos would make her more confident in front of the camera.
  • Early in her career, she was asked to choose between making YouTube videos and pursuing an acting career by her agent, who believed that instead of making videos, she should be interning and auditioning to drive her acting career forward. She decided that she would focus on her YouTube career.
  • She inherited her love of baking from her grandmother, who taught Rosanna baking in her childhood. Right from an early age, Rosanna used to make special treats on request from her friends.
  • She appeared in the music video for the song Dessert by Dawin (ft. Silentó) in 2015.
  • She has revealed in her interviews that it takes about five days to complete the shooting and production of a single episode of her popular YouTube show, Nerdy Nummies.
  • In 2015, she published her cookbook, The Nerdy Nummies Cookbook: Sweet Treats for the Geek in All of Us, which made its debut on the relevant New York Times bestseller list at the fifth position.
  • In 2018, she published her second cookbook, Baking All Year Round.
  • When she started making YouTube videos, she just had an $80 Flip camera to shoot her videos. Also, she had to double up as an editor for her work.
  • In her freshman year at high school, she lettered in varsity soccer and varsity gymnastics.
  • As a part of her education program at the university, she traveled to China, where she managed to earn the title of Educational Goodwill Ambassador from the Tsinghua University.
  • The Italian translation of her first name Rosanna in ‘Rose of Grace.’
  • She worked as an English teacher, during her stay in China, as a part of the global poverty alleviation effort.
  • She has done a lot of activism and endorsement work for the Alzheimer’s Association and has even taken part in the Walk to End Alzheimer’s event, which was organized to raise funds and awareness for the organization.
  • When she got serious about pursuing a YouTube career, she started a small family-run production company with $20,000, which she had saved by keeping aside 10% from each paycheck she had received till then.
  • Early in her teens and youth, she did a number of odd jobs, which included waitressing on and off for 8 years. She also worked at the Abercrombie store, where she was tasked with folding clothes.
  • She co-starred as Scientist (Prof. Beauregard) in the interactive comedy heist film, A Heist with Markiplier, in 2019.
  • In February 2021, she underwent surgery to get her breast implants removed.
  • In 2019, Rosanna was admitted to the hospital for the treatment of a bacterial infection.
  • In December 2019, she publicly announced the death of her father due to leukemia (blood cancer). He had been fighting cancer for 6 years.
  • In March 2021, she celebrated the 1st birthday of her dog Coconut.
  • In 2021, Rosanna hosted and executive produced the baking show Baketopia for HBO Max.
