Honey Boo Boo Looks Radiant in Pic With Pregnant Sister Pumpkin &amp; Niece Ella While Mama June Remains

Publish date: 2024-08-23
Mama June Shannon's daughter Honey Boo Boo is happier than ever as she relishes memorable moments with sister Pumpkin and niece Ella.

Television personality Alana Thompson, known by the stage name Honey Boo Boo is living it up as she embraces life under the custody of her big sister, Lauryn "Pumpkin" Efrid.

A recent photo shared by the teenager proved the "girls" were more than just siblings as they posed together, alongside Pumpkin's daughter Ella Grace Efrid.

HANGING WITH THE GIRLS

The fifteen-year-old shared a photo on Instagram, showing her flanked by her pregnant sister, Pumpkin, and daughter Ella who remained safely tucked in a blue floating vest.

The three women were all smiles as they enjoyed a joyride on a boat. Their excitement was unmistakable, attracting bouts of commendations from fans.

Honey Boo Boo made the post more envy-inducing, referring to Pumpkin and Ella as her "girls for life" via the caption. Sure enough, fans noticed the teenager's acknowledgment of her sister, who became her legal guardian following Mama June's arrest on drug charges.

Most utilized the platform to commend the three divas for always having each other's backs in all circumstances and state that their love for each other is commendable.

RELATIONSHIP WITH MAMA JUNE

While the sisters have learned to stick up for each other through the years, such is hardly the case in their relationship with their mom. The controversial "From Not To Hot" star Mama June has experienced several fallouts with her four daughters in the last decade.

This fallout has led her to become estranged from the quad, including Anna "Chickadee" Cardwell, Jessica "Chubbs" Shannon, Pumpkin, and Honey Boo Boo.

Notably, the star, who has been frolicking in Florida with her boyfriend Geno, remained ousted from her daughters' lives for almost a decade. In fact, all six women reunited for the first time in six years last month in celebration of Pumpkin's baby shower.

While the event seemed like the perfect family reunion, Chickadee clarified to the public days later, that their rift was far from over. According to the 26-year-old, June never apologized for her past mistakes during the reunion. Hence, she had no intention of forcing the relationship.

THE FALLOUT

Fans of the WeTV show are aware of the ongoing feud in June's family, which can be attributed to several occurrences that have happened over the years.

Most of these fallout results from the 41-year old's big mistakes involving substance abuse and wrong romantic choices. The rift between Chickadee and her mom began following June's decision to rekindle her relationship with Mark McDaniel.

McDaniel served jail time for molesting the former as a child. The WeTV star's penchant for brushing off the subject has further strained her relationship with Chickadee.
