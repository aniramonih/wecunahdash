Nic Kerdiles Wife: Was Nic Kerdiles Married?

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Nicolas Kerdiles was a professional ice hockey player from the United States. He was a member of the Anaheim Ducks of the National Hockey League for a short time. The Ducks drafted Kerdiles in the second round of the 2012 NHL Entry Draft.

Kerdiles made his NHL debut for the Ducks on February 22, 2017, against the Boston Bruins. He became the Anaheim Ducks’ first player born in Orange County.

Kerdiles was transferred by the Ducks to the Winnipeg Jets on June 30, 2018, as an impending restricted free agent in exchange for Chase De Leo. On August 21, 2018, he was signed to a one-year, two-way deal by the Jets.
