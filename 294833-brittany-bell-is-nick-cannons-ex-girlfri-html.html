Brittany Bell Is Nick Cannons Ex-girlfriend and a Former Beauty Queen

Publish date: 2024-08-05

Brittany Bell | Brittany Bell, Nick Cannon, and their child | Sources: Getty Images and Instagram.com/missbbell

Nick Cannon's ex-girlfriend, Brittany Bell, is pregnant with their third child together and reportedly the host's tenth child in total. Although it is unclear whether they are a couple again, Cannon and Bell were spotted on vacation in Guam.

Nick Cannon is on his way to becoming a father of ten. He recently made yet another pregnancy announcement about his unborn baby with Brittany Bell.

Cannon surprised his followers with a video posted on Instagram, showing his behind-the-scenes maternity shoot with Bell.

As seen in the clip, the Bell is heavily pregnant and visibly happy to be in Cannon's arms. Bell was sporting different outfits in the shoot, with some barechested poses.

Alongside the video is Cannon's caption, "#Sunshine #SonRISE," possibly indicating that they are expecting a baby boy.

Who Is Brittany Bell?

Although famously known for being the "Masked Singer" host's ex-girlfriend, Bell already had exposure in the entertainment industry as a dancer, model, and beauty queen. After moving to Arizona from Guam, she had her education at Arizona State University with a degree in broadcast journalism.

She became an NBA dancer for the Phoenix Suns while pursuing a career in modeling. However, Bell's beauty goes beyond photographs and runways; she also has a successful history of competing in beauty pageants.

In 2010, Bell was crowned Miss Arizona and represented the state in the Miss USA pageant months later. Unfortunately, she was eliminated from the competition during its first round.

Despite her loss, Bell wasn't afraid to battle it again on the stage. Four years later, she was crowned Miss Guam and represented the state in Miss Universe 2014. Sadly, she had the same fate and did not place in the pageant.

When Did Brittany Bell Date Nick Cannon?

Bell and Cannon's story began in 2015, when they first crossed paths, a year after he separated from his ex-wife, Mariah Carrey. For the next few years, the duo became an on-and-off couple. However, they reportedly called it quits in late 2020 after having kids together.

In 2022, Bre Tiesi confirmed that Cannon had another son with her, Legendary Love. Abby De La Rosa, a professional DJ, is also expecting another child this October.

Despite their apparent relationship status, the "Wild N' Out" star and former beauty queen was spotted celebrating her birthday at Nobu, Malibu, in November that year. An insider later said:

"Nick and Brittany have a complicated relationship, but they're back together and extremely happy. Nick is the only man Brittany loves, and her love for him is very unconditional. They have known each other since they were young, so the connection they have goes deep."

During their years in an on-and-off relationship, Cannon was linked to several other women, but it seems they always found a way back into each other's arms.

In September 2022, Cannon took to his Instagram to share photos on vacation with Bell. The duo went on a trip to Guam and had stunning snaps against the beautiful sunset.

How Many Kids Does Brittany Bell Have with Nick Cannon?

Cannon and Bell are already parents to two children together. They welcomed their firstborn, Golden "Sagon" Cannon, in February 2017 and a daughter, Powerful Queen, in December 2020.

Before having kids of their own, Cannon already welcomed twins Monroe and Moroccan Scott with the "Always Be My Baby" songstress.

He also shares twins, Zion Mixolydian and Zillion Heir, with his ex-girlfriend, Abby De La Rosa, and a son, Zen, with model Alyssa Scott. Sadly, Zen passed away at five months old.
