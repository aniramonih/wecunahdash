Jayla Vlach is an American YouTuber and social media influencer who is a member of the Vlach family of YouTubers that jointly runs the family-friendly vlogging-based YouTube channel titled SmellyBellyTV that has amassed more than 3.5 million subscribers. The channel is often regarded as one of the most-watched in the kids’ category on the platform. On her personal channel titled Its JustJayJay, which just like the parent channel SmellyBellyTV was launched in 2015, she has garnered more than 1 million subscribers. This channel had initially focused only on Minecraft gameplay videos but later, just like the parent channel, it turned completely into a vlogging platform.
