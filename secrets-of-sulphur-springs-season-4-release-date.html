When Will Secrets of Sulphur Springs Season 4 be Released on Disney?

Time travel mystery drama Secrets of Sulphur Springs debuted on Disney Channel in the United States. It was renewed for a third season on February 7, 2022 and its premiere was on March 24, 2023.

The show is set in the fictional Louisiana town of Sulphur Springs and was created by writer Tracey Thomson, who also serves as co-executive producer with Charles Pratt Jr. Keep reading to find out the most up-to-date Season 4 news.

What is the Secrets of Sulphur Springs Season 4 Release Date?

In this series, 12-year-old Griffin Campbell and his family relocate into the closed down and decaying Tremont Hotel in Sulphur Springs, Louisiana that his father bought without much explanation. Thirty years ago, Savannah Dillon, a camper at Tremont Camp mysteriously vanished from the hotel.

Griffin, along with his new best buddy Harper from his new school, travels 30 years into the past to see Savannah Dillon and learn what became of her.

They make an effort to prevent Savannah from vanishing. In Season 2, Harper discovers a portrait of her great-grandmother Daisy which leads her to believe that the answers to her questions about her ties to the Tremont hotel can be found in the 1930s.

As the Campbells get ready to reopen the hotel, paranormal activity rises and Savannah does everything she can to assist Griffin and Harper in learning the hotel’s hidden history. The new mystery family adventure series Secrets of Sulphur Springs premiered on the Disney Channel in the United States. The show airs on Friday nights at 8 o’clock and has a total of 26 episodes spread across three seasons.
