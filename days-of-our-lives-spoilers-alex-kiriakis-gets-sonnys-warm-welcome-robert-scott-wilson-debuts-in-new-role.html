Days of Our Lives Spoilers: Alex Kiriakis Gets Sonnys Warm Welcome Robert Scott Wilson Debuts

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Days of Our Lives (DOOL) spoilers tease that Alexander “Alex” Kiriakis (Robert Scott Wilson) will get Sonny’s warm welcome. Robert Scott Wilson will debut in his new role as these brothers reunite, so let’s talk about Alex’ first airdate and what’s coming up.

As CDL reported, DOOL insiders have been insisting for a while now that Wilson would be playing the part of Alex Kiriakis upon his return to Salem.

Now that Ben Weston (Robert Scott Wilson) and Ciara Brady Weston (Victoria Konefal) have been written off the show together, it frees Wilson up to explore a new opportunity.

While Ben remains happily by Ciara’s side offscreen, Wilson will tackle a different role on Days.

Viewers have never seen an adult version of Alex, so this will give the show a clean slate to work with.

They can mold Alex into whoever they want him to be and give him an interesting personality.

The latest Days spoilers back up this Alex Kiriakis debut and offer a first airdate. On July 27, Sonny will be elated over a reunion with a loved one.

That should be when Alex flies into town and catches up with Sonny.

It probably won’t take long for Victor Kiriakis (John Aniston) to offer Alex a job and perhaps create some tension.

Of course, it’s possible Sonny could be behind that proposition.

Maybe Sonny will pitch the idea of making Alex his COO and unknowingly set the stage for trouble.

Alex just might get power hungry and think he should be the one in the CEO chair eventually.

That could bring a feud between these half-brothers, but Sonny and Alex will start off on a nice note as they come together again during the week of July 25-29.

Regardless, fans will see Robert Scott Wilson make his new debut, so we’ll have to get used to him playing someone besides Ben for a change!

Days of Our Lives spoilers say Alex’s arrival will make some waves sooner or later, so stay tuned to DOOL.
