What Is Brain Jotter Biography and Where Is He From?

Publish date: 2024-08-06

Chukwuebuka Emmanuel Amuzie, also known as Brain Jotter (born February 5th, 1995: Aged 28), is a popular Nigerian comedian and skit maker from Imo State.

Brain Jotter rose to fame through his hilarious comedy skits, which saw him gain massive influence on social media in no time. He started out as a stand-up comedian in his school days before he began making waves on the internet. Today he is counted as one of the most influential skit-makers on social media. Here are important facts about him.

Summary of Brain Jotter’s Bio

  • Full Name: Chukwuebuka Emmanuel Amuzie
  • Nickname: Brain Jotter
  • Gender: Male

  • Age: 28 years old

  • Nationality: Nigeria
  • Sexual Orientation: Straight
  • Religion: Christianity

  • Instagram: @brainjotter

Where Is Brain Jotter From?

Brain Jotter was born on February 5th, 1995, to his parents in Owerri, Imo state, and he hails from the state too. Although he had his basic education in Imo, some sources say he grew up partly in Ghana. This is buttressed by the fact that the comedian studied at Ghana National University.

To clear the air, Brain Jotter is not from Ghana but has only spent some years in the country. After his secondary education, Brain Jotter studied Business Administration at the University of Lagos. Having grown up in different places, Chukwubuka speaks fluently in different languages like French, Kiswahili, Yoruba, Igbo, and English.

He Was a Comedian Back in his School Days

It all started as a hobby because he loved amusing people; Brain Jotter has always been a natural at making people laugh. Back in his school days, he was seen as a comedian and also performed at small school events.

He continued with stand-up comedy even after school and was called occasionally to perform in some shows; however, he was not getting the audience he needed at the time.

From performing at shows, Brain Jotter moved to Instagram and started sharing funny videos on the internet. He would feature some of his friends in his skits and share them on Instagram. He gradually started getting noticed, thereby growing his followers.

His Signature Unperturbed Attitude Helped him attain Stardom

Brain Jotter rose to fame with videos that mostly feature him playing the character of a seemingly unbothered person, an attribute with the connotation ‘I don’t care’ or ‘no stress’ in Nigeria. His face would seem expressionless and cowardly but always ready for the worst.

Furthermore, he is known for being adamant and rude in his videos and also for his sarcastic way of shunning people with the Nigerian phrase ‘Abeg Get Out’.

He would always pick up his shoes and walk away after saying these words. With this unique ability, he gained a lot of positive reactions to his videos, which always had lots of comments once he posted them. Brain Jotter’s major boost was in 2020 when popular celebrities started reposting his skits.

It is recalled that some notable entertainers like Don Jazzy and Tude Ednut reposted his videos which then went viral and gained him more traffic on his page. The comedian posts his skits mainly on Instagram, where he started growing his social media presence. Thanks to his creativity, the few followers on his Instagram account have grown to over two million.

We’re not forgetting other platforms like Facebook and Tik Tok where he has over three million followers, respectively. Brain Jotter, who has become a household name in comedy, has collaborated with many popular comedians and entertainers. Some of them include Oluwadollarz, Viper the Wiper, Mark Angel, Timi Dakolo, and many others.

The Imo-State Born Comic Still Performs in Comedy Shows

The hilarious skit maker has not forgotten his skills as a stand-up comedian. As a matter of fact, he still performs in comedy shows and even organizes his own self-titled shows. Brain Jotter is still organizing campus tours across the country, where he performs alongside other comedians.

He performed at the Sabinus Live in Ghana comedy show in February 2022 with popular skit maker Sabinus and other popular comedians. He has toured several university campuses, including Obafemi Awolowo University (OAU), the University of Calabar, and many others.

Brain Jotter’s Net Worth and How it Accumulated

Brain Jotter has a net worth estimated at $30,000 or ₦13.8 million. This mouthwatering figure accrues from his vast social media presence, which has generated many sources of income for him. With millions of followers across various social media platforms, especially Instagram, Tik Tok, and Facebook, Brain Jotter has become an influencer and does a lot of sponsored posts for individuals and companies.

Brain Jotter charges nothing less than ₦500,000 for his posts. Being a comedian loved by many, he has also bagged many endorsement deals with reputable companies like The Shawn Exchange, Coca-Cola, Hair Solution, etc., and he is paid millions for these endorsements. He also features in advert campaigns for different companies and earns a lot from them.

Brain Jotter also has a massive following on YouTube. His Youtube channel, which has over 700k subscribers, garners millions of views on each skit posted by the comedian. This generates thousands of dollars for him because the video-sharing company pays according to the viewership base.

A Look at Brain Jotter’s Houses and Cars

Brain Jotter loves to spend his money, although he is not known to be extravagant. In May 2020, he made a noble gesture when he surprised his mother with a brand-new Toyota Venza. In 2022, the unique comedian got himself a Ford Kuga said to be worth millions of naira.

He also drives a 2018 Lexus RX 350, ranked 7th in luxury midsize SUVs by the U.S. News. He lives in a luxurious mansion in Lagos.

Is Brain Jotter A Twin?

Brain Jotter is not a twin. The versatile content creator does not openly talk about his family on social media, except for a certain time he opened up about his cousin who went missing and was later reported dead.

He has done many skits with twins or one person playing dual roles. But in real life, it has not been ascertained whether he is a twin or has twin siblings.
