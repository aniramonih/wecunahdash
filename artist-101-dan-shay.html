What else do I need to know about Dan and Shay?

Whilst some people may be unfamiliar with up and coming country-pop duo, Dan and Shay have had a successful few years breaking into the US country scene. With achievements including the release of two hit albums and multiple singles, the pair are continuing to find triumph in their career. As well as a successful run touring with the likes of Hunter Hayes and Blake Shelton, the pair have also pursued their own US and UK tours.
