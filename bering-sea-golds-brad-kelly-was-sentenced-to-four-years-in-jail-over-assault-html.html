Bering Sea Golds Brad Kelly was sentenced to four years in jail over assault

Publish date: 2024-08-11

Bering Sea Gold, the thrilling Discovery series, is back, and it’s brought a lot of drama with it. Viewers recently witnessed a heated brawl between Shawn and Andy in an episode titled “Brawl of Duty.” However, drama isn’t limited to the screen, as Brad Kelly, a member of the Kelly family featured on the show, found himself in hot water last year. Let’s delve deeper into Brad Kelly’s arrest and what it could mean for his future on the show.

The Gold Rush in the Icy Waters of Alaska

Bering Sea Gold, which first aired in 2012, showcases the adventures of various boats as they brave the frigid Alaskan waters in search of gold. Brad Kelly, the head of the Kelly family, arrived in Nome five years ago with his sons, all in pursuit of their dreams of striking it rich.

A Fiery On-Screen Confrontation In a recent episode, tensions ran high as Shawn Pomrenke and Andy Kelly engaged in a physical altercation. The argument between the two escalated, and tempers flared. One viewer took to Twitter to comment on the incident, saying, “Bad luck for Shawn! Was talking all that trash in the car and then called the cops. Great TV though!”

Brad Kelly’s Troubles Off-Screen

Brad Kelly’s troubles extended beyond the on-screen drama. In February 2022, he found himself in a serious predicament. According to TMZ, Brad was arrested following a domestic dispute. Police reports obtained by the publication indicate that Kelly told officers the woman involved had fallen and hit her head on a table. Despite his explanation, he was arrested and charged with second-degree assault involving domestic violence.

Facing Legal Consequences Kelly’s attorney, Jon-Marc Petersen, disclosed that his client had pleaded guilty to felony assault, leading to the dropping of the remaining charges. Brad Kelly was subsequently sentenced to four years in prison, with three of those years suspended. Petersen suggested that, if Kelly maintains good behavior, he might be released after serving eight months behind bars.

Uncertainty Surrounding His Future on Bering Sea Gold While the legal consequences of Brad Kelly’s actions have been determined, his future on Bering Sea Gold remains uncertain. His time in prison could potentially impact his ability to return to the show. However, as of now, the network has neither confirmed nor denied the participation of the Kelly family in future seasons.

Conclusion

The drama on Bering Sea Gold is not limited to the gold-hunting adventures on-screen. Brad Kelly’s arrest and subsequent sentencing have added an unexpected twist to the story. As fans of the show await further developments, it remains to be seen whether Brad will be able to make a comeback in the icy waters of Alaska or if his time behind bars marks the end of his journey on Bering Sea Gold.
