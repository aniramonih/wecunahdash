Who is Jamie-Lee O’Donnell?

Jamie-Lee O’Donnell is an Irish film actress. She is best known for portraying the role of Michelle Mallon in the Northern Irish sitcom Derry Girls.

Early Life, Birth Facts, Parents

Jamie-Lee O’Donnell was born in Derry, Northern Ireland in 1992. In 2022, she celebrated her 30th birthday.

Her parents are not available on the Internet as she has not opened up about them.

She grew up in Derry which is her hometown. Similarly, she might be the single daughter of her parents as she has not talked about anything regarding to her siblings.

She is of Irish nationality and ethnicity. Her birth sign is Pisces.

Education, Academic Qualifications

Jamie-Lee attended St Anne’s Primary School and later joined St Cecilia’s College.

Again, she transferred to North West Regional College for her education. She then attended De Montfort University situated in Leicester.

After that, she studied Performing Arts at the University of Bedfordshire.

Career, Professional Life

Having started her career with minor roles in advertisements and theatre programs later she debuted in the tv series ‘6Degree’ in 2012-2015 followed by another movie ‘Urban & the Shed Crew’ in 2015.

After that, there is no looking backward, and has appeared in more than 10 films to date.

Some of the most notable films she has played include Derry Girls written by Lisa McGee, Tv Series Performance Live in 2018, and this was followed by movies like Doing Money, A Secret Journey, Unwanted, and The Fruit Fix among others.

Besides movies, Jamie has also endorsed various brands such as Silk Fred, ConEire, 20 Stories High, The Goddess Projects, Blush, Amnesty International, and Kevan Jon Fashion.

Salary, Net Worth, Income

Jamie has not shared her financial status with the public. As of 2023, she has an estimated net worth of $1.5 million.

Rumors and Controversies

There are no rumors or controversies about her. Jamie does not tend to be in a limelight and stays away from it.
