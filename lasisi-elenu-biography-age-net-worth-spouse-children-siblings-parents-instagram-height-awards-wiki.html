Early life and education

Lasisi Elenu entered the world on April 20, 1989, in Kano State, Nigeria. He grew up with his parents, Lasisi Afolabi and Madam Grace Osaivbie Afolabi.

After completing his university education, Lasisi Elenu initially pursued a music career. However, he eventually decided to transition into the world of comedy. He has three siblings whose name is unknown to the media.
