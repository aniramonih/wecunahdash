Who is Carmen Pritchett?

Carmen Pritchett is a popular Puerto Rican YouTuber, model, and social media personality. Rising to fame with the YouTube channels Carmen Pritchett, Carmen and Corey, and The Pritchett Family, Carmen Pritchett is married to Corey Pritchett Jr.

Together, the husband and wife duo have amassed over 65 million views on their family channel and over 4.03 million subscribers on their joint channel.

Known for her hilarious prank videos, vlogs, family challenges, and beauty and fashion videos, Carmen Pritchett and her husband share two children.

Quick Facts

Early Life and Childhood

Carmen Pritchett was born on October 16, 1998. She was born in Puerto Rico. As of 2023, she is 25 years old.

Early on in her life, Carmen wanted to join the Air Force. But unfortunately, Carmen had to work several different jobs due to her financial condition.

At 15, Carmen got her first job as a babysitter. After her high school graduation in 2016, she began working in a car plant.

After relocating from Puerto Rico to Los Angeles, California, Carmen Pritchett began her modeling career. However, she only began considering a career as a YouTuber after being introduced to the idea by Corey Pritchett Jr.

Career

Carmen Pritchett launched her solo YouTube channel before starting her family channel. Initially, however, Queen of SSG was unsure what kind of content she wanted to create on her channel.

Nevertheless, she was sure that regardless of the type of content she shared created her own loyal fanbase. So, to figure out what she wanted her channel to focus on, Carmen asked her followers what they wanted to see on her channel.

In an interview, Carmen discussed how she enjoyed making content targeted toward women and said,

“I think it’s important for me to make videos about women because I feel like, with this generation, there isn’t a lot of accessible information related to the difficulties of being a woman, experiencing pregnancy, or being a young mom. So what I do is keep it transparent, showing people that life isn’t always sweet and full of rainbows all the time. Sometimes it’s tough, and sometimes we make mistakes. In my opinion, it’s better to recognize that, do something about it, and learn from it, rather than thinking you’re not allowed to make mistakes in the first place.”

After launching her solo channel in 2018, Queen of SSG also started making videos for her and her family channel. The popular YouTuber, known for the variety of content she shares, talked about her content strategy and said in an interview,

“I like trying different content for different audiences, you know? Like, I’d hate sticking to just one type of content. I feel like that would get boring.”

Furthermore, the mother of two shared how she manages to stay relevant in the ever-growing world of YouTube,

“I like adding my spin to things that are currently trending or something that already went viral.”

Carmen has collaborated with several reputed brands, such as Flat Tummy, Fashion Nova, Savage X Fenty, What Waist, and Baddie Eville.

Talking about her growing empire and her future career plans, Carmen has expressed her desire to take her career in acting and content creation rather than just YouTube.

Carmen Pritchett Husband

Carmen Pritchett is married to YouTuber Corey Pritchett Jr. Corey, born in May 1998, rose to popularity with his and Carmen’s joint channel named Carmen and Corey.

Moreover, Corey also has his solo YouTube channel called CoreySSG. Carmen and Corey met each other for the first time in 2016 and soon began dating.

Although the couple has been married for a long time, Carmen and Corey have expressed their wish to hold a grand celebration celebrating their marriage sometime in the future. The famous YouTube couple, who shares two children, is often the subject of many rumors surrounding their marriage.

According to unverified online sources, Carmen and Corey have been experiencing marital problems for some time now. There have also been many speculations that the pair is in an open marriage.

This rumor originated after Corey shared a video clip where Carmen can be seen straddling an unknown woman as Corey makes his wife and the mystery woman lock lips. Many of the couple’s fans have also expressed concerns about Corey’s possible extramarital affairs.

Children

When it comes to children, Queen of SSG is a mother to two beautiful children. Carmen and Corey welcomed their son CJ in October 2018.

In October 2021, Carmen announced her second pregnancy. Her second son, Cayden Pritchett, was born in March 2022. Apart from this, Carmen is also a devoted stepmother to her stepson, Ayden, who was born in Corey’s prior relationship.

Before the birth of Cayden, Carmen suffered a miscarriage. Although she found out she was pregnant in February 2021, she, unfortunately, revealed in a YouTube video posted in April 2021 that she had lost her baby.

In a video titled “We Lost Our Baby.”, Carmen emotionally shared,”I had a miscarriage. I’ve been miscarrying for two weeks. Didn’t know…” Moreover, she revealed that although she had been feeling “different” for quite some time, the news of her miscarriage came “out of nowhere” and that it was “unexpected” news.

In addition, Carmen described how she felt during her short-lived pregnancy and said,

“This pregnancy has been completely different from my first one… Extremely different. I just want to be in bed, lay down…have no energy.”

Although the YouTube couple expressed that they definitely wanted a more prominent family in the future, owing to the mishap, they weren’t going to put any pressure on themselves to have a baby as soon as possible.

Social Media

Carmen Pritchett YouTube

Carmen Pritchett is a popular YouTuber with over 1.04 million subscribers. Her personal channel, Carmen Pritchett, was created on May 31, 2018, is mainly catered to the female audience, and contains many videos related to fashion, beauty, makeup, motherhood, and pregnancy.

Apart from this, Carmen also has a joint YouTube channel with her husband, Corey Pritchett Jr. Their channel, Carmen and Corey, started in November 2016, has accumulated over 4.03 million subscribers and has a total of 549,657,243 lifetime views.

Moreover, Queen of SSG also runs a family channel with Corey named The Pritchett Family. With over 1.06 million subscribers, the married couple shares videos about their family life, daily vlogs, videos featuring their kids, and parenthood experience.

Carmen Pritchett Instagram

With over 4.7 million followers, Queen of SSG is active on Instagram under the username “carmenpritchett.” The YouTube personality is known for her bold and unabashed personality on Instagram, and as a result, she has amassed quite an impressive following.

Carmen shares several sexy pictures and videos of herself on Instagram. Likewise, she also posts many pictures with her husband and children.

Along with this, the Puerto Rican beauty has also collaborated with many famous brands on her Instagram, including Gentle Oasis, Waist Snatchers, What Waist, Savage X Fenty, and Baddie Ville.
