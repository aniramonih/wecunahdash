Jan Luis Castellanos Plays Diego Torres On Netflix's '13 Reasons Why'

The fourth season of Netflix's 13 Reason Why was one of the highly anticipated television series of 2020, and it released on 5 June 2020.

The series that circulates the storyline amongst the complicated teenagers have now a new member, Jan Luis Castellanos. Jan portrayed the role of Diego Torres, who is a footballer and has a crush on a lady named Jessica Davis.

Jan Luis Castellanos as Diego Torres on Netflix's '13 Reasons Why' (©: Instagram/Jan Luis Castellanos)

The character of Diego Torres is shown with the struggles with his own anger and emotions. In the series, Diego Torres is a friend of Monty, his dead friend. It shows that Diego battles with the loss of his friend and team up with Winston to know the reason behind Monty's death.

Till the date, Jan Luis Castellanos has made his appearance in three episodes of 13 Reasons Why. We will have to wait for the next episode to see what happens to his character in the Netflix television series.

Who Is Jan Luis? His Career Accolades

Jan Luis Castellanos is a young Dominican actor. Born in 1995 in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic, Jan's birthday is on 11 September.

As a child, he grew up under the care of his grandparents, with whom he moved to New Jersey in the United States when he was eight years old.

Jan has three siblings in his family, and he is the eldest offspring of his parents. He attended Secaucus High School, from where he got into the theatrical musicals like 'West Side Story' & 'Jersey Boys.' He got into modeling during his college years and later pursued his career in the entertainment industry.

Jan Luis Castellanos has starred in several movies and tv shows, including Depression and Insanity (2018), Runaways (2018), and 13 Reasons Why (2020) to this date.
