Who Is Samuel Harness The Voice? Meet Him On Instagram

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Who is Samuel Harness? The singer has already left the judges speechless with his performance on the blind audition of The Voice season 21 and fans have been wondering to know more about the talented musician.

Harness’ singing caught the attention of all judges but it was John Legend who got was the most impressed of all and even stated that he was jealous of his angelic voice. The three coaches, John, Blake, and Ariana turned their chairs to bring him on their team however he ultimately chose to be in Team Legend.

Samuel Harness Family

Samuel’s parents revealed on the show that the Harness family adopted him and 5 other children after they couldn’t have children of their own. Nevertheless, he got into a very nice family with a father and mother who even inspired him to get into music.

Harness expressed that his life changed after he met his birth mother at the age of 18 years. He was dealing with an identity issue that his adoptive parents couldn’t understand but he had a sense of understanding after meeting his biological mom.

The Voice participant is a father to a 4-year-old boy and claims that he learned a lot about fatherly love after the birth of his own child. He even thanked his parents for all the love and care they have presented to him throughout his life that has made him a better man.

Samuel Harness Age

Samuel is currently 26 years of age. The young singer wasn’t able to stay with his birth parents and got adopted by his parents when he was a small boy. Even though he is adopted he always felt enormous love from his parents apart from some identity issue.

Harness’ too his identity issues on his mind and started making music to pour out his feelings. Despite that, the singer is forever grateful for the love he has received from his parents and the encouragement for him to pursue a career in music.

Samuel Harness Wikipedia

Samuel Harness doesn’t have a Wikipedia page yet but he became quite popular after his appearance on the famous music show. The singer got into music from an early age due to his father who is a member of a bluegrass group known as The Harness Family Band.

Harness released his single “Tuesday” on iTunes in 2017 and till now he has shared many of his own songs on the online music store. He is yet to charm the audiences with his performance on the main stage of the competition.

Meet Samuel Harness On Instagram

Samuel is on Instagram as @samuel_harness.  He has over 11 thousand followers on his page and has shared over 100 posts.

The singer is very passionate and his passion for music can be seen through his IG posts where he is often seen with his guitar. Samuel also loves to share pictures of his adorable son whose name he hasn’t revealed yet.
