Jada Pinkett Smith Pets - Celebrity Pets

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Indo, Gracie, Zhaki, Tyson (Dog)

Breed: Rottweilers

Will Smith and Jada Pinket Smith have five Rottweilers. The dogs’ names are Indo, Gracie, Zhaki, and Tyson. It’s not known what the 5th Rottweiler’s name is. These dogs also received training from Cesar Millan, who is a good friend with Jada and gifted her Indo. Jada took in one of the Rotwiellers from a friend. The other two were gifts to Will from Jay Leno. It’s unknown how many of these dogs are still alive, as neither Will nor Jada has mentioned or posted about them since the mid-2010s.

Rocco (Dog)

Jada Pinkett Smith had a dog named Rocco, who sadly passed away from a rattlesnake bite in 2014. After, Cesar Milan (who Jada is a good friend with) trained her and her dogs on how to avoid snakes and reptiles.

Bandit (Bandito) (Dog)

Breed: French Bulldog

In 2020, for Jada Pinket Smith’s 49th birthday, she was gifted a French bulldog puppy which she name Bandit, aka Bandito. This dog was a gift from her good friend and celebrity dog trainer Cesar Millan.

Fang (Dog)

Lucco (Dog)

Breed: Mixed Breed | Adopted Aug 2023

In August 2023, Jada Pinkett shared the news that the Smith family adopted a rescue pup named Lucco.

Pero (Dog)

Breed: Poodle

Jada Pinkett Smith’s first pet was a black standard Poodle named Pero.

My Aunt Karen sent me this pic of myself and my cousin Brett this morning. These were the days I would torture my cousins to participate in plays that I would make up and perform at family gatherings. The poor animals had to join also. The dog in the pic was my first pet…a Standard Poodle named Pero:)
He was an amazing dog.

Jada Pinkett Smith, Facebook, March 2015

Jada Pinkett Smith

Birthday

September 18, 1971 (52)

Jada Pinkett Smith is an American actress, screenwriter, producer, talk show host, and businesswoman. She’s best known for co-hosting the talk show Red Table Talk, which won her a Daytime Emmy Award. Her notable TV and film roles include A Different World (1991–1993), The Nutty Professor (1996), The Matrix Reloaded (2003), The Matrix Revolutions (2003), Gotham (2014–2016) and Girls Trip (2017).

Jada Pinkett Smith is married to Will Smith and have two kids together: Jaden and Willow. The couple also shares four Rottweilers.
